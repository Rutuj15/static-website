5 Awesome Email Ideas for the Next 30 Days

1. Celebrate the bond of siblings on Raksha Bandhan

Its an Indian festival celebrated on August 31st, 2023, where people cherish the unique relationship between brothers and sisters. 

 

On this occasion, you can talk about your bond with your siblings and the value of family. Gradually transition to your offering and show how your products/services can contribute to cherishing these relationships.

2. Acknowledge and reward the hard work of employees on Labor Day

It's celebrated primarily in the US and Canada on the first Monday in September (September 4, 2023) to honor the Labor movement's contributions.

 

You can create an email that encourages your audience to take a break and relax. Highlight products or services that can contribute to a relaxing day, such as spa treatments, comfortable loungewear, or entertainment options.

3. Celebrate the guiding lights on Teachers' Day!

It's observed in India on September 5, 2023, to express gratitude to the mentors who illuminate our paths of knowledge. 
 

Celebrate this occasion by providing a special discount on existing educational courses, if you have any, and highlighting the teachers who created them, as shown below.

4. Raise your mugs, and lets cheer on Oktoberfest!

It is a lively German festival celebrated from September 16th to October 3rd, 2023, where friends and family gather, have fun, eat, and drink beer.

 

Most food and beverage industries take advantage of this fest by running special discounts on food items and drinks commonly consumed during this occasion.

 

You can also get creative by incorporating motifs of Oktoberfest in the email template, as shown below.

5. Embrace the cozy vibes of autumn/fall

Fall typically starts around mid-September in the Northern Hemisphere. It's a time to revel in the serene beauty of this season. 

 

When we think of fall, we imagine earthy browns, yellowing leaves, pumpkin spice lattes, and apple pie. Use these colors and motifs to inject some of the fall aesthetic into your email templates.

EMAIL TEMPLATES FOR INSPIRATION

  • App launch video email template - Showcase your new app with a captivating video through email, driving excitement and user interest.

 

  • App waitlist email template -  Invite users to join a waitlist for an upcoming app and encourage them to sign up for early access.

 

  • Telegram community invite email template - Invite users to a Telegram community, offering access to exclusive connections and conversations.

 

  • Crypto newsletter email template - Offer tailored cryptocurrency updates, drawing users to the website for more engaging content.
