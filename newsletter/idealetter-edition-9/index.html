AMA Corner 

Welcome to the AMA corner, where we invite different experts or influencers in the field of email marketing for an AMA session. You can ask your questions or doubts about campaign strategies, AMP technology, or any other related topics.
 

This month, we have Arnav, the Founder & CEO of Inagiffy, an end-to-end newsletter agency answering a burning question you might have. Arnav has helped hundreds of clients create and send engaging and effective newsletters that drive results. Here is one of the questions he answered:

Q: Ravi asks I have an audience of 150k and I send them email newsletters regularly, but my open rate wont increase > 5%. How can I improve this?

A: Hi Ravi, this is Arnav. A low open rate can result from poor domain reputation, irrelevant content, or lack of personalization. Ill help you fix your metrics with the below simple steps: 

1) Check your domain health if you see anything but green, you must run warmup campaigns.

2) Check your past newsletter content to see if there are issues with the image size etc. which could be a one-way ticket to spam.

3) Segment your audience into active and inactive subscribers and send your newsletters to the active ones first. This will improve your domain health and deliverability, and prevent landing in spam or being marked as promotions in your inbox. 

4) Test everything - You can also test different subject lines, preview texts, and send times to see what works best. I hope this helps you increase your open rate and engage your subscribers.

 

Hope this helps answer your question Ravi!

Note: Ensure you have an unsubscribe link at the bottom of the email.
