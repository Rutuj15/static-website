How Aiza's New Onboarding Flow Generated 2x More Revenue

Wednesday, Oct 16, 2024

03 PM -

04 PM

This event has been completed

Did you know that companies with optimized onboarding see up to 86% higher customer retention rates?

And if even a 5% increase in customer retention can increase profits by 25–95%, imagine what a well-crafted onboarding strategy could do for your business.

Which is why we have Aiza Coronado, sharing how she achieved a 2x revenue boost with a smart, data-driven approach to onboarding.

In this exclusive interview, you’ll find:

  • Actionable steps to assess and improve your onboarding journey

  • An honest look into what worked, and what didn't, from Aiza’s own experiments

  • Ways to run experiments and find the "sweet spots" to maximize impact.


Join us and discover how to turn your onboarding process into a powerful growth engine.

Aiza Coronado

Co-founder, CaaSocio
