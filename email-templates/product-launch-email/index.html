    What is a product launch email?

    Getting the word out before you unveil a new product is essential to help your customers grasp how it works.

    A customer-centric product launch helps you gather essential feedback early on, which you can use for product updates. It also helps you take more informed decisions about future products. With our product launch email template, you can generate buzz for its release and show your audience how the latest features add value to their life by adding dynamic content, carousels, accordions, and image galleries. The email template looks great visually, prompting users to buy your product.

    Features of Mailmodo's product launch email template

    • Visually pleasing and eye-catching product imagery.

    • The color scheme blends in with the overall email design.

    • Compelling and clear CTA button.

    • Responsive for mobile and desktop.

    • The template follows all the email accessibility guidelines.

    • Automatically added a fallback version for email clients who don't support AMP emails.

    How to customize this email template?

    Follow these steps to customize these templates:

    • Click on the 'Use this Template' button.

    • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's template builder if you already have an account. If you don't have one, you can create one by clicking here.

    • In the email template builder, you can try different email layouts, add or remove content blocks, play around with fonts and colors, and interactive widgets such as emoji ratings, polls, quizzes, dropdown menus, etc.

    • We recommend selecting from our premade content blocks to save time and effort.

    • Once you're finished editing, preview the email template on mobile and desktop. We also offer AMP and HTML versions of the email.

    • Then, save the email template so that you can use it for future campaigns.
