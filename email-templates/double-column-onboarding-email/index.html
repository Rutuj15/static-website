    What is Double Column Onboarding Email?

    A Double Column Onboarding Email is an email marketing communication designed with a two-column layout for the content. This type of email typically divides the content into two side-by-side columns, allowing for the presentation of information, images, and other elements in a visually appealing and organized manner.

    Double Column Onboarding Emails are often used in user onboarding sequences, where businesses introduce new users or customers to their products, services, or platforms. The two-column layout can be used to provide a balance of text and visuals, making it easier for recipients to understand and engage with the information.
