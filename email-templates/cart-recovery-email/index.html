    What is a cart recovery email?

    Cart recovery emails can help you recover lost sales by reminding and engaging the abandoned cart customers and giving them a compelling offer to complete their transaction. Customers abandon their cart for many reasons, and as per Beymard's Institute research, the major reasons are

    • High shipping cost - 48%

    • Create an account to complete the purchase - 24%

    • Delivery period too long - 22%

    Knowing the reason behind the abandoned cart, you can tailor your email to the recipient's pain point and give them relevant incentives to convert them. Our cart recovery email template is designed to help you recover your lost revenue by giving users a compelling design and offer right inside their inbox.
