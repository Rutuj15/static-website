    What is an Invoice reminder email template?

    An invoice reminder email template is a pre-designed email format that businesses use to send reminders to their customers about outstanding/due payments. It typically includes details about the unpaid invoice, payment due date, and a friendly reminder to settle the payment.

    The template helps businesses streamline the process of following up on overdue invoices and encourages prompt payment from customers.

    We understand how daunting it can be to stay on top of your finances. That's why Mailmodo created an efficient and effective solution to help remind your clients of their outstanding invoices. Whether you're a small business owner or a freelancer, the Invoice follow-up Email Template can help you save time and improve your cash flow.

    Chasing payments can be a hassle and thus our invoice email can save time for you by automating the entire process. With just a few clicks, you can send polite and professional invoice reminder email to your clients, ensuring that you get paid on time. With its neutral tone and general domain, this product is suitable for a wide range of industries and purposes.

    Here's the text version of the follow up invoice email:

    Invoice Email Template

    Hi (Recipient's name),

    This is a friendly reminder regarding the invoice for [invoice ref number] with a balance of [invoice balance].

    Please ensure the payment is made by [invoice due date]. If you have already taken care of it, kindly ignore this message.

    If you need any assistance, feel free to contact us.

    Kindest regards,

    (Your name)
