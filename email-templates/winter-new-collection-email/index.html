    What is Winter New Collection Email?

    A Winter New Collection Email is an email marketing communication that businesses use to introduce and promote their latest collection of products specifically designed for the winter season. These emails showcase winter-themed clothing, accessories, or items that are suitable for colder weather and align with the trends and styles of the season.

    Winter is among the most exciting and profitable seasons for business as it comes bearing festivities and holidays such as Thanksgiving, Cyber Monday, Black Friday, Christmas, Halloween, New year, and the list goes on.

    The holiday season gives people a reason to celebrate and grab their favorite products/services at a discounted price, win exciting offers, enter into lottery and giveaways, go on a picnic with their families, host barbeque in their backyard, and so on.

    Marketers have this huge opportunity to leverage this season to engage, nurture, and convert their new customers and retain their loyal ones. A well-crafted email marketing strategy is crucial to do that and you cannot capture attention without the right messaging, and design. That's where our winter season email templates come in.

    Whether you want to run a giveaway on Christmas, give exclusive deals to your valuable customer, run a clearance sale, or launch your new collection, we have a template for each use case.

    Features of Mailmodo's winter new collection email template

    • Structure your winter email messaging using different layouts such as single-column, double-column-, inverted pyramid, etc.

    • Add interactive AMP email widgets such as spin the wheel, polls, quizzes, etc., to engage readers.

    • Eye-catching email banner with clear headline and sub-headlines.

    • Multiple options to choose the right call-to-action button that attracts recipients' attention.

    • Our templates are responsive to every screen size. You can just how different elements render on a smaller screen from our email template editor.

    • The template follows all the email accessibility guidelines.

    • If you add any interactive AMP widget, a fallback version will be added automatically for email clients who don't support AMP emails.

    • Integrate your Zapier, Intercom, Mixpanel, Shopify, Webhook, Facebook, Freshsales, Zendesk, etc. with Mailmodo and unlock advanced features of our template builder.

    How to customize this email template?

    Follow these steps to customize these templates:

    • Click on the ' Use this Template' button.

    • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's template builder if you already have an account. If you don't have one, you can create one by clicking here.

    • In the email template builder, you can try different email layouts, add or remove content blocks and blocks, and interactive widgets such as polls, spin the wheel, carousels, etc.

    • We would recommend selecting from our premade content blocks to save time and effort.

    • Once you're finished editing, preview the email template on both mobile and desktop. We also let you see how the email will look in AMP-supported email clients.

    • Then, save the email template so that you can use it for future campaigns.
