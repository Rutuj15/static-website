    What is the scholarship application form?

    If your institute is looking for more students to apply for the scholarship, this scholarship application email form is best for you. You can collect data from all the eligible students through this form and shortlist candidates that are best suited to receive the scholarship.

    The application form is embedded in the email so students can fill in all the required details without any redirects to the browser. You can customize it by adding or removing questions per your application process.

    Features of Mailmodo's scholarship application email template

    • Interactive embedded application form allowing recipients to submit the forms within the email.

    • Select from pre-made form blocks to save time and effort.

    • Light colors that grab attention.

    • Personalization in the email copy.

    • The template is responsive to every device.

    • Templates are created considering the accessibility guidelines.

    • Automatically added a fallback version for email clients who don't support AMP emails.

    • Add personalized fields in the subject line and within the email too.

    • Collect survey responses directly in your app by integrating it with Mailmodo, including Customer.io, Calendly, Zoom, MoEngage, and more.

    Check out all the integration offered by Mailmodo

    How to customize this email template

    Follow these steps to customize this template:

    • Click on the 'Use this Template' button.

    • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's email template builder if you already have an account. Otherwise, you'll be asked to sign up to customize the template.

    • In the email template builder, you can try out different email layouts, add or remove content blocks, and add interactive widgets like polls.

    • To save time and effort, we recommend selecting from our pre-made content blocks.

    • Once you're finished with the editing, preview the email template on mobile and desktop. We also let you see how the email will look in AMP-supported email clients.

    • Then, save the email template so that you can use it for future campaigns.
