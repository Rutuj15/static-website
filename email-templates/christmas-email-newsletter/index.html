    What is a Christmas email Newsletter?

    Don't miss out on this opportunity to make your email marketing stand out this holiday season!

    Designed with the holiday spirit in mind, this Newsletter is the perfect tool for businesses and individuals alike to send personalized Christmas emails that truly stand out. With customizable text fields and festive graphics, this Newsletter allows you to easily tailor your message to your audience, whether you're sending holiday promotions to your customers or sending warm Christmas wishes to friends and family.

    This Newsletter is specifically designed for the holiday season and includes customizable sections for text and images. It is compatible with major email marketing platforms, making it easy to send your message to your audience.

    Features of Mailmodo's Christmas Email Newsletter

    • Eye-catching colors and visuals

    • Prominent CTA button.

    • Personalization in the email copy.

    • Interactive spin the wheel widget to engage recipients and allow them to play games within the email.

    • Responsive for mobile and desktop.

    • The Newsletter follows all the email accessibility guidelines.

    How to customize this email Newsletter?

    Follow these steps to customize these Newsletters:

    • Click on the 'Use this Newsletter' button.
    • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's Newsletter builder if you already have an account. If you don't have one, you can create one by clicking here.
    • In the email Newsletter builder, you can try different email layouts, add or remove content blocks and blocks, and interactive widgets such as polls, spin the wheel, carousels, etc.
    • We would recommend selecting from our premade content blocks to save time and effort.
    • Once you're finished editing, preview the email Newsletter on both mobile and desktop. We also let you see how the email will look in AMP-supported email clients.
    • Then, save the email Newsletter so that you can use it for future campaigns.
