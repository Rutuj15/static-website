What is Sendinblue(Brevo) Email Template?

Sendinblue Email Templates are pre-designed email templates designed to be visually appealing and responsive, ensuring that the emails adapt and display correctly on different devices and email clients. Additionally, Sendinblue provides features for segmentation, personalization, automation, and A/B testing, allowing users to optimize their email campaigns for better engagement and results. Using Sendinblue Email Templates can save time and effort in the email creation process.

How to use Sendinblue(Brevo) Email Template?

Once logged in, locate the option to create a new email campaign or access the email template editor. This may vary depending on the Sendinblue interface and version you are using. In the email template editor, you'll find a selection of pre-designed templates to choose from. Browse through the available options and select a template that suits your campaign or message. Once you have chosen a template, you can customize it to match your branding and campaign needs. The email template editor will provide options to modify various elements, such as text, images, colors, fonts, and layout. Make changes to the template to align it with your brand identity and campaign message.

Although Sendinblue(Brevo) Email Templates created in Mailmodo cannot be exported and used directly in Sendinblue(Brevo), they can still provide users with valuable inspiration to create similar templates from scratch. However, creating email templates from scratch can be a daunting and time-consuming task.

To save your precious time, it is best to use the email templates available in Mailmodo to send your email marketing campaigns, rather than starting from scratch. You can even edit these templates in the email template builder and send them from Mailmodo to achieve higher deliverability and improved email marketing performance.

How to customize this email template?

Follow these steps to customize these templates:

  • Click on the 'Use this Template' button.

  • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's template builder if you already have an account. If you don't have one, you can create one by clicking here.

  • In the email template builder, you can try different email layouts, add or remove content blocks and blocks, and interactive widgets such as polls, spin the wheel, carousels, etc.

  • We would recommend selecting from our premade content blocks to save time and effort.

  • Once you're finished editing, preview the email template on both mobile and desktop. We also let you see how the email will look in AMP-supported email clients.

  • Then, save the email template so that you can use it for future campaigns.
