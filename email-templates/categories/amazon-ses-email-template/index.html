What is Amazon SES Email Template?

Amazon SES (Simple Email Service) is a cloud-based email service that allows businesses to send transactional and marketing emails to their customers. The pre-built templates and drag-and-drop builder make it easy to create custom email designs, while the dynamic content features can increase engagement and conversions. Additionally, Amazon design email templates offers support and resources to help you optimize your email campaigns for better engagement and conversion rates.

How to use Amazon SES Email Template?

To use Amazon SES Email Template first go to the AWS Management Console and log in to your account. Navigate to the Amazon SES console and click on "Email Templates" on the left-hand menu and then click on the "Create a Template" button to create a new email template. Now you will be presented with a form where you can enter the name and description of your template. After naming your template, choose a layout from the available options. Amazon SES provides pre-designed layouts for various types of emails, such as newsletters, promotions, and event invitations. You can choose the layout that best suits your needs.

Although Amazon SES Email Templates created in Mailmodo cannot be exported and used directly in Amazon SES, they can still provide users with valuable inspiration to create similar templates from scratch. However, creating email templates from scratch can be a daunting and time-consuming task.

To save your precious time, it is best to use the email templates available in Mailmodo to send your email marketing campaigns, rather than starting from scratch. You can even edit these templates in the email template builder and send them from Mailmodo to achieve higher deliverability and improved email marketing performance.

How to customize this email template?

Follow these steps to customize these templates:

  • Click on the 'Use this Template' button.

  • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's template builder if you already have an account. If you don't have one, you can create one by clicking here.

  • In the email template builder, you can try different email layouts, add or remove content blocks and blocks, and interactive widgets such as polls, spin the wheel, carousels, etc.

  • We would recommend selecting from our premade content blocks to save time and effort.

  • Once you're finished editing, preview the email template on both mobile and desktop. We also let you see how the email will look in AMP-supported email clients.
