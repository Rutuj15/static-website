What is Zoho Email Template?

Zoho Email Templates refer to pre-designed email templates that are available within Zoho Mail or other Zoho applications. Zoho Email Templates can be accessed within Zoho Mail or other Zoho applications that offer email functionality, such as Zoho Campaigns or Zoho CRM. Zoho Email Templates are ready-to-use designs that enable users to create visually appealing and engaging emails for various purposes. They provide a convenient starting point and customizable foundation, helping businesses save time and effort in email creation while maintaining a professional appearance.

How to use Zoho Email Template?

Log in to your Zoho Mail account or the Zoho application that offers email functionality, such as Zoho Campaigns or Zoho CRM. Within the email interface, click on the option to create a new email or start a new campaign, depending on the specific application you are using. In the email editor, look for the option to choose a template. Zoho provides a gallery of pre-designed email templates for various purposes. Browse through the available templates and select the one that best suits your needs. Once you have chosen a template, you can customize it to align with your branding and campaign objectives.

Although AWeber Email Templates created in Mailmodo cannot be exported and used directly in AWeber, they can still provide users with valuable inspiration to create similar templates from scratch. However, creating email templates from scratch can be a daunting and time-consuming task.

To save your precious time, it is best to use the email templates available in Mailmodo to send your email marketing campaigns, rather than starting from scratch. You can even edit these templates in the email template builder and send them from Mailmodo to achieve higher deliverability and improved email marketing performance.

How to customize this email template?

Follow these steps to customize these templates:

  • Click on the 'Use this Template' button.

  • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's template builder if you already have an account. If you don't have one, you can create one by clicking here.

  • In the email template builder, you can try different email layouts, add or remove content blocks and blocks, and interactive widgets such as polls, spin the wheel, carousels, etc.

  • We would recommend selecting from our premade content blocks to save time and effort.

  • Once you're finished editing, preview the email template on both mobile and desktop. We also let you see how the email will look in AMP-supported email clients.
