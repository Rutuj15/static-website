What is gamification email template?

Gamification Templates are designed to add an interactive and engaging element to your email marketing campaigns. This gamified approach not only captures attention but also increases engagement, click-through rates, and conversions. With Gamification Templates, businesses can send unique and engaging games inside an email that stand out in the inbox and leave a lasting impression on recipients.

These templates incorporate games such as:

  • Spin-to-Win
  • Knock Down
  • Shuffle Cups
  • Claw Machine, etc

By leveraging these interactive elements, you can increase click rates, boost engagement, and leave a lasting impression on your subscribers. Whether it's enticing them with a virtual wheel spin or challenging them to knock down objects, Gamification Email Templates offer a unique way to captivate your audience and make your emails stand out. With their interactive design, these templates provide a fun and memorable experience that can drive higher click-through rates and conversions.

How to use gamification templates?

  • Interactive games in emails entice recipients to play the games, resulting in higher click-through rates.
  • Unique and engaging gamification experiences leave a lasting impression, increasing brand awareness.
  • Fun and exciting gamification elements foster a positive association and enhance customer loyalty.
  • Gamification differentiates your emails from traditional ones, capturing attention and increasing brand recall.

How to customize and send these templates?

You can customize these templates using our codeless, drag-and-drop email editor.

  • Pick your template and click the Use this template button.
  • Mailmodo's email template builder will open. Here, you can customize the template:
  • Try out different email layouts, and add or remove content blocks.
  • Add your email copy, and adjust the heading, subheading, and white spacing.
  • Choose interactive elements from our AMP widget gallery such as forms, surveys, polls, reviews and ratings, carousels, quizzes, etc.
  • Dont forget to customize the game as per your needs. Add the rewards you want to offer you customers when they play the game.
  • Once you make all the changes, preview both the AMP and fallback (HTML) versions and check for mobile and desktop previews.
  • Then, save the template.

You can now send the email template to the recipient.
