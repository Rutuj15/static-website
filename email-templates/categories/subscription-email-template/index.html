What are subscription emails?

Subscription emails are used to attract new customers to subscribe to your newsletter, book club, or other subscription-based offerings. These emails are essential for growing an audience interested in your latest news, products, or community events. They highlight the value of subscribing, such as exclusive content, updates, or special offers, encouraging potential customers to stay connected and engaged with your brand over time.

What makes our subscription email templates special?

Here are a few interactive widgets that make our subscription email templates special:

- Chip selection- Suppose you want to know through which channel did they discover about your business. you can use chip selection to let subscribers choose from options like social media, search engines, word of mouth, or other channels—all within the email. This interactive feature gives you valuable insights quickly, without directing users to an external survey.

- Forms- Gather valuable information by embedding forms directly in your email. For example, you could ask, “What do you expect from this newsletter?” This can help you to understand subscriber preferences and provide the content that resonates best with your audience.
