What is Salesforce Email Template?

Salesforce Email Templates can sometimes be the simplest approach for completing your emails. They make it simpler by quickly composing and sending emails with merge fields from data stored in Salesforce like Contacts, Leads, Opportunities, or Custom Objects.

When you have a lot of contacts or leads in Salesforce, it might be difficult to determine who needs to be alerted of new information. You can send a single email to each of these contacts or prospects at once by using Salesforce email templates.

Salesforce Classic, Lightning Experience, and Email Template Builder are the three types of Salesforce email templates. You can use all three email template types within an org.

How to use Salesforce Email Templates?

Using Salesforce Email Template is easy you can go to their App launcher then you can either create your own email template or you can select from the pre-available email templates. Salesforce HTML Email Templates are usually used in order to create email templates faster and more easily with visual tools. Email Templates in Salesforce can save lots of time for users in sending emails because of their productivity feature.

Although Salesforce Email Templates created in Mailmodo cannot be exported and used directly in Salesforce, they can still provide users with valuable inspiration to create similar templates from scratch. However, creating email templates from scratch can be a daunting and time-consuming task.

To save your precious time, it is best to use the email templates available in Mailmodo to send your email marketing campaigns, rather than starting from scratch. You can even edit these templates in the email template builder and send them from Mailmodo to achieve higher deliverability and improved email marketing performance.

How to customize this email template?

Follow these steps to customize these templates:

  • Click on the 'Use this Template' button.

  • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's template builder if you already have an account. If you don't have one, you can create one by clicking here.

  • In the email template builder, you can try different email layouts, add or remove content blocks and blocks, and interactive widgets such as polls, spin the wheel, carousels, etc.

  • We would recommend selecting from our premade content blocks to save time and effort.

  • Once you're finished editing, preview the email template on both mobile and desktop. We also let you see how the email will look in AMP-supported email clients.

  • Then, save the email template so that you can use it for future campaigns.
