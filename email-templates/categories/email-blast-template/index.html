What are Email Blast templates?

An email blast template is used for simultaneously sending a single email message to a large group of recipients.

This type of email is often used for marketing campaigns, announcements, newsletters, or any communication intended to reach a broad audience. A well-crafted email blast template helps ensure consistency in branding, messaging, and layout.

What makes our email blast templates special?

1. Games inside emails:

  • Games are added to the emails to encourage participation by offering discounts or rewards. For example, a 'spin-the-wheel' or a whack-a-mole game.

Here are some types of email blasts where games can be effective:

  • Product Launch or Promotion Spin-the-Wheel: Subscribers spin a wheel to unlock a discount, freebie, or special offer.

  • Seasonal Campaigns Holiday-Themed Quizzes: Create quizzes related to the season (e.g., Which holiday dessert are you? or How well do you know Halloween?) with incentives like a discount at the end.

2. Add-to-cart widgets:

In case of clearance sale emails or product launches, you can add an "add-to-cart" widget which can let customers add the sale products directly to their carts without any redirects.

3. An interactive form:

Interactive forms can collect valuable user data such as names, email addresses, preferences, or survey responses. For example, if you send an email blast for webinar registrations, you can add a form directly in the email, allowing recipients to sign up without leaving their inbox. This makes the process seamless, improving conversion rates as users can easily register with just a few clicks.
