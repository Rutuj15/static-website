What is quiz in email template?

A quiz in email is an interactive gamification element that hooks readers and encourages them to answer the questions. And at the end, they can see their score and get to unlock exciting rewards. Such a fun and joyful experience make quizzes unique and a great tactic to boost email engagement and build brand loyalty.

Everyone likes a little challenge from time to time, and quiz email campaigns help users test their knowledge within the email.

How to use quiz emails?

  • Share coupon codes based on users' scores in the quiz.
  • Trivia quizzes such as harry potter quizzes, star wars quizzes.
  • An assessment quiz assesses users' understanding of your product, services, or industry.
  • Get leads via adding relevant quiz questions.

Learn more: How to Use Email Gamification to Make Emails More Entertaining

Why should you use Mailmodo's quiz email templates?

  • Customize quiz questions for different campaigns in our drag-and-drop template editor.
  • Create engaging and interactive quizzes without any coding skills.
  • Save time by inserting a pre-made quiz widget from the builder or template library.
  • Add relevant questions with images in the tabs of the widgets. Set the right answers by simply selecting them.
  • Customize your results tab by adding content and images to the configured score.

How to customize and send these templates?

You can customize these quiz email templates using our codeless, drag and drop email editor.

  • Pick your template and click on the 'Use this template' button.

  • Mailmodo's email template builder will open. Here, you can customize the quiz template:

    • Add or remove questions and select the right options.
    • Add compelling images, GIFs, and emojis to make the quiz visually appealing.
    • Add interactive elements from our AMP widget gallery, such as polls, review and rating options, etc.

  • Once you make all the changes, preview both the AMP and fallback (HTML) version and check for mobile and desktop previews.

  • Then, save the template.

You can now send the email template to the recipient.
