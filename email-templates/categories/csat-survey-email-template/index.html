What is customer satisfaction (CSAT) survey email template?

A customer satisfaction (CSAT) survey is a questionnaire that helps you understand your customers' satisfaction level with your product/services, customer support, and brand. You can analyze satisfaction levels using different CSAT surveys - Net promoter score (NPS) survey, Customer effort score (CES) survey, etc.

Data collected through these surveys can help your brand in different ways - update products/services, optimize websites for better user experience, improve customer services, identify opportunities to grow, etc.

How to use CSAT survey emails?

You can use the CSAT survey to collect feedback for different aspects of your business:

  • Ask users to rate your onboarding process.

  • Collect feedback on your product/services.

  • Collect feedback on how likely they'll recommend your product/services to their peers, family members, friends, etc.

  • Ask users to mention any issues and bugs in your product/services.

  • Ask users to rate your latest product launch.

  • Ask users to rate your customer service experience.

  • Collect feedback on your website's performance and design

Why should you use these CSAT survey email templates?

  • Add interactive rating widgets such as thumbs-ups, emojis, and star ratings.

How to customize and send these CSAT survey templates?

You can customize these templates using our codeless, drag and drop email editor.

  • Pick your template and click on the 'Use this template' button.

  • Mailmodo's email template builder will open. Here, you can customize the template:

    • Change the email layout and content alignment.

    • Add or remove content blocks.

    • Choose the form widget you want to add - Star rating, number rating, thumbs up rating, etc.

    • You can also add opinion polls and quizzes to make interactive email experiences.

  • Once you make all the changes, preview both the AMP and fallback (HTML) version and check for mobile and desktop previews.

  • Then, save the template.

You can now send the email template to the recipient.
