What is Mailgun Email Template?

Mailgun Email Template is a pre-designed email template that you can use to create and send professional-looking emails via the Mailgun email delivery service. Mailgun is an email service provider that offers email APIs and other tools to help businesses send and manage their email communication.

Mailgun Email Templates are created using HTML and CSS code and can include images, text, buttons, and other elements to help businesses create visually appealing and engaging email messages.

How to use Mailgun Email Templates?

Once you have created an account, you can choose a Mailgun Email Template that suits your needs. Mailgun offers a wide range of templates for different purposes, such as promotional emails, newsletters, and transactional emails. After selecting a template, customize it to fit your needs. You can add or remove text, images, buttons, and other elements to make the template your own.

Although Mailgun Email Templates created in Mailmodo cannot be exported and used directly in Mailgun, they can still provide users with valuable inspiration to create similar templates from scratch. However, creating email templates from scratch can be a daunting and time-consuming task.

To save your precious time, it is best to use the email templates available in Mailmodo to send your email marketing campaigns, rather than starting from scratch. You can even edit these templates in the email template builder and send them from Mailmodo to achieve higher deliverability and improved email marketing performance.

Each template follows email accessibility guidelines

How to customize this email template?

Follow these steps to customize these templates:

  • Click on the 'Use this Template' button.

  • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's template builder if you already have an account. If you don't have one, you can create one by clicking here.

  • In the email template builder, you can try different email layouts, add or remove content blocks and blocks, and interactive widgets such as polls, spin the wheel, carousels, etc.

  • We would recommend selecting from our premade content blocks to save time and effort.

  • Once you're finished editing, preview the email template on both mobile and desktop. We also let you see how the email will look in AMP-supported email clients.

  • Then, save the email template so that you can use it for future campaigns.
