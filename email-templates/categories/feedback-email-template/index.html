What is feedback email template?

Feedback emails collect opinions and reviews from a group of recipients regarding your product and services, events, team members, marketing campaigns, etc.

These emails help you understand your audience better and a much deeper level. Using this information, you can build stronger relationships, offer content valuable to them, and build brand loyalty.

How to use feedback emails?

  • Ask for feedback post events, webinars, conferences, etc.
  • Ask users to rate their experience so far with your brand using NPS or CSAT surveys.
  • Collect feedback on your product's new feature launch.
  • Collect feedback regarding your customer support team.
  • Ask users to rate your latest blog posts.
  • Feedback after a demo call.
  • Feedback survey to collect reviews from new customers.
  • Ask users to assess certain features of your product/services.

How to customize and send these templates?

You can customize these templates using our codeless, drag and drop email editor.

  • Pick your template and click on the 'Use this template button.

  • Mailmodo's email template builder will open. Here, you can customize the template:

    • Try out different email layouts, alignment, and content blocks, and play around with premade blocks.
    • Add your email copy, and adjust the heading, subheading, and white spacing.
    • Choose interactive elements from our AMP widget gallery such as forms, surveys, polls, reviews and rating, carousels, quizzes, etc.

  • Once you are done with the changes, preview both the AMP and fallback (HTML) version and check for mobile and desktop previews.

  • Then, save the template.

You can now send the email template to the recipient.
