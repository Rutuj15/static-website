What is an Iterable Email Template?

Iterable email template is a reusable blueprint for your email communications. You can use them to establish content, aesthetics, and layout that you can apply to many campaigns while maintaining the credibility and consistency of your messaging. By creating and reusing templates, you can ensure consistency in your email communication and focus more on the content of your messages rather than the formatting. In Iterable, you can create five types of templates: email, in-app, push notification, SMS, and web push.

How to use Iterable Email Template?

An iterable email template is a pre-designed email that can be used as a starting point for multiple email campaigns with different content. You can choose a template and then customize it according to your needs. Iterable makes a duplicate of the template you select to use for a campaign when you choose to utilize it. The campaign's content may be changed in this manner without altering the original template.

Although Iterable Email Templates created in Mailmodo cannot be exported and used directly in Iterable, they can still provide users with valuable inspiration to create similar templates from scratch. However, creating email templates from scratch can be a daunting and time-consuming task.

To save your precious time, it is best to use the email templates available in Mailmodo to send your email marketing campaigns, rather than starting from scratch. You can even edit these templates in the email template builder and send them from Mailmodo to achieve higher deliverability and improved email marketing performance.

How to customize this email template?

Follow these steps to customize these templates:

  • Click on the 'Use this Template' button.

  • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's template builder if you already have an account. If you don't have one, you can create one by clicking here.

  • In the email template builder, you can try different email layouts, add or remove content blocks and blocks, and interactive widgets such as polls, spin the wheel, carousels, etc.

  • We would recommend selecting from our premade content blocks to save time and effort.

  • Once you're finished editing, preview the email template on both mobile and desktop. We also let you see how the email will look in AMP-supported email clients.

  • Then, save the email template so that you can use it for future campaigns.
