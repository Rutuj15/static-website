What is calculator email template?

Embedding a calculator in email acts as a engaging and interactive element that captures the recipients' attention. It also helps reciepients by making it easy to perform financial calculations, savings estimations, or ROI assessments, which aids in decision-making, and increasing overall engagement and conversions. Make emails stand out in the crowded inbox, resulting in a more memorable and impactful email marketing campaign.

These email templates incorporate calculators such as:

  • EMI calculator
  • BMI Calculator
  • SIP Calculator
  • Email ROI Calculator
  • NPS Calculator

The versatility of calculators makes them adaptable to various industries, providing valuable tools and enhancing the overall email experience for recipients. Also, the interactive element not only increases engagement but also can boosts conversions. No more switching apps or reaching for your phone when you need to crunch some numbers.

Who can use this?

Calculators in email can be beneficial for a wide range of industries like FinTech, Insurance, Real Estate, and Health and Fitness to enhance their email marketing campaigns.

Why should you use Mailmodo's calculator in email?

  • Higher engagement: Calculators in the email entice people to interact with the email, and calculate the numbers.

  • New experience: Create positive word of mouth because of the memorable email experience.

  • Boost conversions: After using the calculator, they would also be tempted to check out the offering.

How to customize and send these templates?

You can customize these templates using our codeless, drag-and-drop email editor.

  • Pick your template and click the Use this template button.
  • Mailmodo's email template builder will open. Here, you can customize the template:
  • Try out different email layouts, and add or remove content blocks.
  • Add your email copy, and adjust the heading, subheading, and white spacing.
  • Choose interactive elements from our AMP widget gallery such as forms, surveys, polls, reviews and ratings, carousels, quizzes, etc.
  • Don't forget to customise the calculators to your brand's requirements.
  • Once you make all the changes, preview both the AMP and fallback (HTML) versions and check for mobile and desktop previews.
  • Then, save the template.

You can now send the email template to the recipient.

To learn in detail, check out our help guide.

Each template follows email accessibility guidelines

  • Legible font size and type.
  • Color contrast follows the standard 4.5:1 ratio.
  • Enough white space and smaller paragraphs for better readability.
  • Distinguishable and clear call to action button.
  • ALT text for all the images.

Ensures proper inbox rendering

  • Responsive for every screen size.
  • AMP display for AMP-supported email clients.
  • HTML display if email clients don't support AMP.
  • Email subject line and preheader text preview.
