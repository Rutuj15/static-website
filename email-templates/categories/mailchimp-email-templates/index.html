What is Mailchimp Email Template?

A template is a preset layout that you build your campaign on. Mailchimp Email Templates provides you with many template options, but you can also create your own template to match your brand.

Mailchimp offers a wide range of templates for different types of emails, including newsletters, promotional email templates, event invitation email templates, and more. The templates are designed to be easy to use, with drag-and-drop editing tools and customizable content blocks. Designers can also use Mailchimp's template language to create a dynamic template they can import as HTML.

How to use Mailchimp Email Templates?

The Mailchimp Email Templates are simple to use. Simply go to their campaigns and choose one of the pre-existing email templates or create your own. Create Campaigns by clicking on the drop-down menu next to the template you want to use, the campaign builder will open where you can make the necessary changes to the chosen email template and then it will be ready to go!

Although Mailchimp Email Templates created in Mailmodo cannot be exported and used directly in Mailchimp, they can still provide users with valuable inspiration to create similar templates from scratch. However, creating email templates from scratch can be a daunting and time-consuming task.

To save your precious time, it is best to use the email templates available in Mailmodo to send your email marketing campaigns, rather than starting from scratch. You can even edit these templates in the email template builder and send them from Mailmodo to achieve higher deliverability and improved email marketing performance.

How to customize this email template?

Follow these steps to customize these templates:

  • Click on the 'Use this Template' button.

  • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's template builder if you already have an account. If you don't have one, you can create one by clicking here.

  • In the email template builder, you can try different email layouts, add or remove content blocks and blocks, and interactive widgets such as polls, spin the wheel, carousels, etc.

  • We would recommend selecting from our premade content blocks to save time and effort.

  • Once you're finished editing, preview the email template on both mobile and desktop. We also let you see how the email will look in AMP-supported email clients.

  • Then, save the email template so that you can use it for future campaigns.
