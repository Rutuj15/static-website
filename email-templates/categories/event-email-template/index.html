What is event email template?

Event Email Templates are for the people who wish to reach out and greet their audience on festivals/holidays plus capitalize on widely-celebrated events. These Festival Templates are designed in a way that will increase the open rate thus increasing your email deliverability and most importantly a great way to put a smile on your consumer's face during any festive/holiday season. There are different types of Occasions Email Templates for which you can send emails, including:

  • Holiday season sale
  • Festive occasion greetings and discounts

The purpose behind event email is to greet and build a long-lasting relationship with your clients by offering them the festive discount and offerings you have specially made for them. The templates included in the Event email category have more interactive elements for your audience to engage and have fun plus each template I altered keeping the festive or holiday vibe in mind.

How to use event emails?

  • Wish your audience for the upcoming occasion with greeting email templates.
  • Increase your deliverability and engagement because of interactive email templates (spin-the-wheel, quiz, etc).
  • Sending your love and wishes through your sweet offerings.
  • Get more sales using these event email templates.

How to customize and send these templates?

You can customize these templates using our codeless, drag-and-drop email editor.

  • Pick your template and click the Use this template button.
  • Mailmodo's email template builder will open. Here, you can customize the template:
  • Try out different email layouts, and add or remove content blocks.
  • Add your email copy, and adjust the heading, subheading, and white spacing.
  • Choose interactive elements from our AMP widget gallery such as forms, surveys, polls, reviews and ratings, carousels, quizzes, etc.
  • Once you make all the changes, preview both the AMP and fallback (HTML) versions and check for mobile and desktop previews.
  • Then, save the template.

You can now send the email template to the recipient.
