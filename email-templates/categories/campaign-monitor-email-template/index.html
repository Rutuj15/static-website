What is a Campaign Monitor Email Template?

Campaign Monitor Email Templates is a feature offered by the email marketing service provider Campaign Monitor. It allows users to create and customize email templates that can be used for their email campaigns. Campaign Monitor offers a variety of pre-designed templates that users can choose from and customize to their liking. Alternatively, users can create their templates from scratch using Campaign Monitor's drag-and-drop email builder, which requires no coding skills.

How to use Campaign Monitor Email Template?

To use Campaign Monitor Email Template you need to login into your account and then click on the "Templates" tab in the navigation menu. Then choose a pre-designed template that you want to use for your email campaign. You can also browse through the available templates or use the search bar to find a specific template.

Although Campaign Monitor Email Templates created in Mailmodo cannot be exported and used directly in Campaign Monitor, they can still provide users with valuable inspiration to create similar templates from scratch. However, creating email templates from scratch can be a daunting and time-consuming task.

To save your precious time, it is best to use the email templates available in Mailmodo to send your email marketing campaigns, rather than starting from scratch. You can even edit these templates in the email template builder and send them from Mailmodo to achieve higher deliverability and improved email marketing performance.

How to customize this email template?

Follow these steps to customize these templates:

  • Click on the 'Use this Template' button.

  • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's template builder if you already have an account. If you don't have one, you can create one by clicking here.

  • In the email template builder, you can try different email layouts, add or remove content blocks and blocks, and interactive widgets such as polls, spin the wheel, carousels, etc.

  • We would recommend selecting from our premade content blocks to save time and effort.

  • Once you're finished editing, preview the email template on both mobile and desktop. We also let you see how the email will look in AMP-supported email clients.

  • Then, save the email template so that you can use it for future campaigns.
