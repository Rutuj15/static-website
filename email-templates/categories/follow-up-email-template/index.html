What is a follow-up email template?

A follow-up email template is a pre-designed message to stay in touch with the contact/subscribers after an initial contact or action.

Types of follow-up email templates

1. Onboarding follow-up email: These emails are sent after a customer has purchased or expressed interest in a product to check in and see if they need help or have any feedback.

2. Free trial follow-up: These emails are sent after users sign up for a free trial to remind them of the product or service's benefits and encourage them to convert to paid users.

3. First-purchase follow-up: Thank customers for their initial purchase and encourage repeat business with offers, discounts, or product suggestions based on their previous orders.

4. Event follow-up: After an event, send attendees a thank you note. Include a summary of the event, ask for feedback, and offer a way to stay in touch.

5. Feedback follow-up email: Acknowledges and expresses gratitude for customer feedback, emphasizing the importance of encouraging future communication.

6. Post-meeting follow-up: Email after a meeting to summarize what was discussed, confirm the next steps, and express appreciation for the person's time.

Follow-up Email Subject Lines

  • "Don’t Miss Out: Our Latest Updates Inside"

  • "Following Up on Our Recent Conversation"

  • "Quick Check-In: Any Questions?"

  • "Reconnecting: How Can We Help?"
