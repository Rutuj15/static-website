What is Sendy Email Template?

Sendy is a self-hosted email marketing platform that allows businesses to send email campaigns to their subscribers at a lower cost than other email service providers. Sendy Email Templates are pre-designed templates that can be used to create email campaigns with a professional look and feel.

Sendy Email Templates are created using HTML and CSS and can be customized to suit the needs of the business and create visually appealing emails. One of the benefits of using Sendy Email Templates is the cost savings. Since Sendy is a self-hosted email marketing platform, businesses can save money by hosting the platform on their own servers instead of paying a monthly subscription fee to a third-party email service provider.

How to use Sendy Email Templates?

While Sendy itself doesn't provide built-in email templates, you can use third-party email templates with Sendy. Search for or create email templates that fit your desired style and purpose. You can find email templates from various sources, including online marketplaces, template libraries, or by hiring a designer to create custom templates for you. Ensure that the templates you choose are compatible with Sendy's email template requirements (usually HTML and CSS-based templates).

Although Sendy Email Templates created in Mailmodo cannot be exported and used directly in Sendy, they can still provide users with valuable inspiration to create similar templates from scratch. However, creating email templates from scratch can be a daunting and time-consuming task.

To save your precious time, it is best to use the email templates available in Mailmodo to send your email marketing campaigns, rather than starting from scratch. You can even edit these templates in the email template builder and send them from Mailmodo to achieve higher deliverability and improved email marketing performance.
