What is GetResponse Email Template?

GetResponse is an email marketing platform that provides various tools and features to help businesses create, send, and track their email campaigns. GetResponse Email Templates are pre-built email templates that users can utilize to create visually appealing and professional-looking emails for their marketing and communication purposes. Using GetResponse Email Templates can help you save time and effort in creating professional-looking emails for your business.

How to use GetResponse Email Templates?

Once you are logged in, navigate to the "Emails" tab or section in the GetResponse dashboard. Look for the option to create a new email or choose an existing email campaign. In the email creation or editing interface, you will find a gallery of pre-designed email templates. Browse through the available templates and choose the one that best suits your needs. Once you have selected a template, you can start customizing it. Use the editor's tools to adjust the layout, change colors, and customize the styling to match your brand's identity.

Although GetResponse Email Templates created in Mailmodo cannot be exported and used directly in GetResponse, they can still provide users with valuable inspiration to create similar templates from scratch. However, creating email templates from scratch can be a daunting and time-consuming task.

To save your precious time, it is best to use the email templates available in Mailmodo to send your email marketing campaigns, rather than starting from scratch. You can even edit these templates in the email template builder and send them from Mailmodo to achieve higher deliverability and improved email marketing performance.

How to customize this email template?

Follow these steps to customize these templates:

  • Click on the 'Use this Template' button.

  • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's template builder if you already have an account. If you don't have one, you can create one by clicking here.

  • In the email template builder, you can try different email layouts, add or remove content blocks and blocks, and interactive widgets such as polls, spin the wheel, carousels, etc.

  • We would recommend selecting from our premade content blocks to save time and effort.

  • Once you're finished editing, preview the email template on both mobile and desktop. We also let you see how the email will look in AMP-supported email clients.

  • Then, save the email template so that you can use it for future campaigns.
