What is an Announcement Email Template?

An announcement email is an email notification sent to tell customers about something new, updated, or changed in your business. This type of email is essential for keeping your customers, clients, or subscribers up-to-date with the latest developments and ensuring they are informed about important changes that may affect them.

What makes these templates special?

“Add to cart” widget:

Simplify the buying process by embedding an "Add to Cart" widget in your emails, allowing customers to add products directly to their cart without leaving the email.

Ideal for various announcement emails, including:

  • New product launches

  • Back-in-stock alerts

  • Product updates

This interactive feature boosts engagement and makes the path to purchase more efficient.

Forms:
Use forms in announcement emails to gather feedback on new launches. This can help you to make data-driven decisions

Countdown timers:
Countdown times in announcement emails create a feeling of FOMO among your customers.

By counting down to the exact moment, a product becomes available, you build excitement and hype. Customers feel they are part of an exclusive event, eagerly awaiting the launch.

Carousels:
Carousels add an interactive element to your emails, enhancing customer engagement and providing more visual information. Use them to:

  • show customer reviews in the back-in-stock emails.
  • Provide a 360-degree view of products on each slide of the carousel.
