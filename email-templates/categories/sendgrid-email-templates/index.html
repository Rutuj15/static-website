What are SendGrid Email Templates?

SendGrid's Email Templates give each user the ability to create their own templates or choose from the pre-built email templates for each of the emails they need to send.

SendGrid Email Templates can be used for a variety of purposes, from newsletters and promotional emails to transactional emails like order confirmations or password resets. The platform also offers tools for testing and previewing emails, as well as detailed analytics to track the success of email campaigns.

How to use SendGrid Email Templates?

In order to use SendGrid Email Template you need to navigate to the "Templates" section. Click on "Create Template" to get started. You can either choose a pre-designed template or create a custom one from scratch.

Overall, using SendGrid Email Templates is a straightforward process that allows you to create professional-looking emails quickly and easily.

Although Sendgrid Email Templates created in Mailmodo cannot be exported and used directly in Sendgrid, they can still provide users with valuable inspiration to create similar templates from scratch. However, creating email templates from scratch can be a daunting and time-consuming task.

To save your precious time, it is best to use the email templates available in Mailmodo to send your email marketing campaigns, rather than starting from scratch. You can even edit these templates in the email template builder and send them from Mailmodo to achieve higher deliverability and improved email marketing performance.

How to customize this email template?

Follow these steps to customize these templates:

  • Click on the 'Use this Template' button.

  • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's template builder if you already have an account. If you don't have one, you can create one by clicking here.

  • In the email template builder, you can try different email layouts, add or remove content blocks and blocks, and interactive widgets such as polls, spin the wheel, carousels, etc.

  • We would recommend selecting from our premade content blocks to save time and effort.

  • Once you're finished editing, preview the email template on both mobile and desktop. We also let you see how the email will look in AMP-supported email clients.

  • Then, save the email template so that you can use it for future campaigns.
