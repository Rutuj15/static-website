What are onboarding emails?

An onboarding email is sent to new users or customers to welcome them and guide them through the initial steps of using a product or service. These emails are crucial for setting the tone for your relationship with new users and ensuring they have a smooth start.

An effective onboarding email template typically includes a warm welcome message, clear instructions for getting started, and an overview of key features or benefits. It may also outline the next steps to encourage engagement, provide contact information for support, and include a friendly reminder to share feedback with an interactive form embedded in the emails.

Types of Onboarding Emails

Welcome Emails: Initial email welcoming new users to your service.

Product Tour Emails: Emails that introduce users to the main features and benefits of the product.

Feedback Request Emails: Emails asking for feedback on the onboarding process to improve user experience.

Account Setup Guide: Help users set up their accounts or complete essential first steps with an email

What Makes Our Onboarding Email Templates Special?

Interactive Forms:

A form embedded in the email saves customers time by allowing them to complete tasks without leaving their inbox. This streamlined experience enhances user convenience and boosts engagement.

Perfect for onboarding emails, such as:

  • Collecting user preferences
  • Gathering feedback on initial experiences
  • Confirming account details or subscriptions
