What is Klaviyo Email Template?

Klaviyo email templates are pre-designed layouts that businesses can customize to fit their brand and messaging. Klaviyo HTML Email Templates are for your email and SMS marketing needs and give you access to 100+ expertly built templates for email, SMS, forms, and automation—all easily customizable in our content editor. Klaviyo Email Templates can save you time and effort compared to designing an email from scratch, and they also have a range of email templates for different types of emails, such as newsletters, promotional emails, abandoned cart reminders, and more.

How to use Klaviyo Email Templates?

Creating an email campaign in Klaviyo is a simple process. Just navigate to the Campaigns tab, click 'Create Campaign', and select 'Email'. From there, you can choose a pre-designed template by category or search for a specific one. To help guide you through the process and create effective email campaigns, Klaviyo offers a wealth of resources and tutorials on its website.

Although Klaviyo Email Templates created in Mailmodo cannot be exported and used directly in Klaviyo, they can still provide users with valuable inspiration to create similar templates from scratch. However, creating email templates from scratch can be a daunting and time-consuming task.

To save your precious time, it is best to use the email templates available in Mailmodo to send your email marketing campaigns, rather than starting from scratch. You can even edit these templates in the email template builder and send them from Mailmodo to achieve higher deliverability and improved email marketing performance.

How to customize this email template?

Follow these steps to customize these templates:

  • Click on the 'Use this Template' button.

  • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's template builder if you already have an account. If you don't have one, you can create one by clicking here.

  • In the email template builder, you can try different email layouts, add or remove content blocks and blocks, and interactive widgets such as polls, spin the wheel, carousels, etc.

  • We would recommend selecting from our premade content blocks to save time and effort.

  • Once you're finished editing, preview the email template on both mobile and desktop. We also let you see how the email will look in AMP-supported email clients.

  • Then, save the email template so that you can use it for future campaigns.
