    What is a discount offer email?

    Discount offer emails are one of the best promotional emails you can send to boost your sales and drive more revenue to your business. Everyone likes a little discount as they get a product/service at a bargain. These emails are also beneficial to drive sales from dormant or inactive users by giving them an enticing offer they can't ignore.

    You can use our discount offer email template for different campaigns, such as during the festive season or a flash sale. Customize it to your needs and see your sales increase with our discount email template.

    Features of Mailmodo's discount offer email template

    • Interactive form embedded in the email allowing recipients to submit it within the email.

    • Brand's color creating brand awareness and identity.

    • Visuals that catch users' attention.

    • Discount offers are placed prominently in a compelling manner.

    • Dropdown menus to give users options to choose from.

    • Prominent and clear CTA button.

    • The template is responsive to every device.

    • Templates are created considering the accessibility guidelines.

    • Automatically added a fallback version for email clients who don't support AMP emails.

    • Add personalized fields in the subject line and within the email too.

    • Collect survey responses directly in your app by integrating it with Mailmodo, including Customer.io, Zoho CRM, WebEngage, MoEngage, and more.

    How to customize this email template

    Follow these steps to customize this template:

    • Click on the 'Use this Template' button.

    • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's email template builder if you already have an account. Otherwise, you'll be asked to sign up to customize the template.

    • In the email template builder, you can try out different email layouts, add or remove content blocks, and add interactive widgets like polls.

    • To save time and effort, we recommend selecting from our pre-made content blocks.

    • Once you're finished with the editing, preview the email template on mobile and desktop. We also let you see how the email will look in AMP-supported email clients.

    • Then, save the email template so that you can use it for future campaigns.
