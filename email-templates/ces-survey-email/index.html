    What is the CES survey email?

    Customer effort score is an important metric that tells you how much effort your customers put into completing tasks such as raising a query, purchasing, etc. The higher the CES, the better customer experience and thus higher customer retention.

    The formula to calculate CES is = Sum of your CES survey sent / Total response received. So, if the total score is 100 and you got 50 responses, then CES would be 2.

    You can measure CES by sending a survey to your customers at the right time, especially immediately after they have resolved their query, completed a purchase, had a demo call, etc. It increases your chances of hearing back from them as they just took action, and they might be happy to share their feedback.
