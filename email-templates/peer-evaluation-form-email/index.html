    What is a peer evaluation form email?

    Peer evaluation form emails help you relay feedback in a professionally, timely, and clearly.

    It offers new perspectives to the employer which they can use to improve their employee experience in the workplace. You can ask a combination of close and open-ended questions to get personal recommendations and suggestions from your employees. They will feel valued knowing that their insights and contributions matter. Eliminate paperwork by collecting and managing peer reviews online with our peer evaluation form email template. Your employees can respond to quizzes, forms, surveys, and rating requests inside the email to avoid any redirects.

    Features of Mailmodo's peer evaluation email template

    • Embedded evaluation form within the email to let recipients fill and submit the form without leaving their inbox.

    • In-mail dropdown and emoji rating widgets.

    • Responsive for mobile and desktop.

    • The template follows all the email accessibility guidelines.

    • Automatically added a fallback version for email clients who don't support AMP emails.

    • Collect feedback response directly in your app by integrating it with Mailmodo, including HubSpot, Customer.io, Zoom, Calendly, WebEngage, MoEngage, etc.

    Follow these steps to customize these templates:
