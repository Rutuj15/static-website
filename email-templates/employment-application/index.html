    What is an employment application email?

    If your company is looking to hire new talent to expand and grow, then our employment application email template is perfect for you. While reaching out to shortlisted prospects and directing them to a browser to complete the form might result in the drop-off. But, with our email template, you can embed the form within the email. This will help applicants to fill in all their details without leaving the inbox, thus reducing the chances of drop-offs.

    Besides, you can add interactive widgets such as polls, dropdown menus, calendars to book a call, etc., and make the application process even more interactive.

    Features of Mailmodo's employment application email template

    • Interactive embedded application form allows recipients to fill and submit the form within the email without redirecting.

    • Eye-catching colors with a neat email design.

    • Dropdown menu to make it easy for the recipient to select an option.

    • Embedded calendar to let the recipient select their date of birth.

    • The template is responsive to every device.

    • Notable CTA button.

    • Templates are created considering the accessibility guidelines.

    • Automatically added a fallback version for email clients who don't support AMP emails.

    • Add personalized fields in the subject line and within the email too.

    • Collect survey responses directly in your app by integrating it with Mailmodo, including WebEngage, Calendly, Zoom, MoEngage, and more.

    Check out all the integration offered by Mailmodo

    How to customize this email template

    Follow these steps to customize this template:

    • Click on the 'Use this Template' button.

    • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's email template builder if you already have an account. Otherwise, you'll be asked to sign up to customize the template.

    • In the email template builder, you can try out different email layouts, add or remove content blocks, and add interactive widgets like opinion polls, dropdowns, chip buttons, etc.

    • To save time and effort, we recommend selecting from our pre-made content blocks.

    • Once you're finished editing, preview the email template on mobile and desktop. We also show how the email will look in AMP-supported email clients.

    • Then, save the email template for future email campaigns.
