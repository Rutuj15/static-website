    What is an invite email template?

    Invitation emails are used to invite people to participate in your event, be it a webinar, workshop, podcast, or seminar. The email should add all the important details such as the topic to be discussed, the list of speakers, timing, venue, and the reason for attending, etc., to get more event registrations.

    This Airmeet invite email contains an embedded form that interested recipients can fill out to register for the event within their inbox. You can use a Zapier integration to add the registrants directly to Airmeet when they fill out the form.

    Features of Mailmodo's Airmeet invite email template

    • Single email layout and eye-catching email design.

    • In-mail event confirmation without any redirects to a browser.

    • The template is responsive to every device.

    • Big and noticeable CTA button.

    • Templates are created considering the accessibility guidelines.

    • Automatically added a fallback version for email clients who don't support AMP emails.

    • Collect feedback responses directly in your app by integrating it with Mailmodo, including Salesforce, Freshmarketer, Calendly, Clevertap, Zoho CRM, Freshsales, MoEngage, and more.

    How to customize this email template

    Follow these steps to customize this email template:

    • Click on the 'Use this Template' button.

    • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's email template builder if you already have an account. Otherwise, you'll be asked to sign up to customize the template.

    • In the email template builder, you can try out different email layouts, add or remove content blocks, and add interactive widgets like opinion polls, dropdowns, chip buttons, etc.

    • To save time and effort, we recommend selecting from our pre-made content blocks.

    • Once you're finished editing, preview the email template on mobile and desktop. We also show how the email will look in AMP-supported email clients.

    • Then, save the email template for your future email campaigns.
