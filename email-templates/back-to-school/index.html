    What is a Back-to-School Template?

    The Back-to-School Email Template is a strategic communication tool for engaging customers during the back-to-school shopping season. This template efficiently displays the range and quality of school supplies available, resulting in a virtual storefront that buyers can browse from the comfort of their own homes. This email template is more than simply a promotional message; it's a call to customers to prepare for the new school year with your high-quality, low-cost school supplies. The pattern also emphasizes the sale's limited-time character, pushing buyers to act quickly and get their favorite things before they sell out.

    Features of Back-to-School Email Template:

    1. Eye-catching visuals

    2. Clear call-to-action buttons.

    3. Gamification widgets to increase interactions.

    4. A single email layout with a neat design.

    5. Responsiveness for both mobile and desktop viewing.

    6. The template follows all the email accessibility guidelines.

    7. Easy customization to align with your brand’s aesthetics and values.

    How making your emails interactive can benefit you?

    • Higher Engagement - Calculators in the email entice people to interact with the email, and calculate the numbers.
    • New Experience - Create positive word of mouth with memorable email experiences.
    • Boost Conversions - After using the calculator, they would also be tempted to check out the offering.

    How to customize this email template?

    Follow these steps to customize these templates:

    • Click on the 'Use this Template' button.
    • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's template builder if you already have an account. If you don't have one, you can create one by clicking here.
    • In the email template builder, you can try different email layouts, add or remove content blocks and blocks, and interactive widgets such as polls, spin the wheel, carousels, etc.
    • We would recommend selecting from our premade content blocks to save time and effort.
    • Don't forget to customise the calculators to your brand's requirements.
    • Once you're finished editing, preview the email template on both mobile and desktop. We also let you see how the email will look in AMP-supported email clients.
    • Then, save the email template so that you can use it for future campaigns. Check out our help guide to know in detail.
