    What is an enquiry form email?

    Enquiry forms allow you to collect information on prospective buyers visiting your website, connecting with you on social channels, or through communities. Besides marketers, teachers, healthcare professionals, real estate agents, and educational institutes can also use this form to collect basic information about the individual who shows interest in their services.

    You can collect their contact details, family background, the reason for contacting you, etc., and then reach out to them.

    Our enquiry form email template is customizable. You can add forms, surveys, and interactive widgets to collect all sorts of information by creating a seamless email experience for the recipient.

    Features of Mailmodo's enquiry form email template

    • Interactive enquiry AMP email form embedded allowing recipients to submit it within the email.

    • Single-column email layout to create a better email experience.

    • In-mail calendar that allows recipients to choose the date as per their preference.

    • Thumbs-up rating widget to collect feedback.

    • Outstanding and clear CTA button.

    • The template is responsive to every device.

    • Templates are created considering the accessibility guidelines.

    • Automatically added a fallback version for email clients who don't support AMP emails.

    • Add personalized fields in the subject line and within the email too.

    • Collect survey responses directly in your app by integrating it with Mailmodo, including Customer.io, Calendly, Zoom, Intercom, Clevertap, HubSpot, and more.

    Check out all the integration offered by Mailmodo

    How to customize this email template

    Follow these steps to customize this template:

    • Click on the 'Use this Template' button.

    • You will be redirected to Mailmodo's email template builder if you already have an account. Otherwise, you'll be asked to sign up to customize the template.

    • In the email template builder, you can try out different email layouts, add or remove content blocks, and add interactive widgets like polls.

    • To save time and effort, we recommend selecting from our pre-made content blocks.

    • Once you're finished with the editing, preview the email template on mobile and desktop. We also let you see how the email will look in AMP-supported email clients.

    • Then, save the email template so that you can use it for future campaigns.
