What is BigMailer.io Transactional Email Service?

BigMailer offers a transactional email service that allows users to consolidate their promotional and transactional emails on one platform. With a single price point, users can manage all their email campaigns and leverage a unified customer view with real-time engagement based targeting. Additionally, users have the flexibility to bring their existing HTML templates or use BigMailer's drag-n-drop editor to build mobile optimized templates. With features like syncing customer records with various apps and integrations, BigMailer provides an efficient and customizable solution for managing transactional emails.
