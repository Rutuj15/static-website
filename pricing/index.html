Everything you need to know about the AMP email idea generator.

Mailmodo enables but is not limited to creating and sending of AMP emails. You can create, send and automate both AMP and HTML email campaigns without any tech intervention.

AMP email is an open-source framework created by Google that allows emails to render and display dynamic elements like accordions, carousels, forms, calendars, shopping carts, dynamic APIs, etc. It creates a mini-website inside an email.

AMP emails are just like any other emails except the fact that they come with several superpowers. Not every email client renders AMP emails though. At present, AMP email is supported by Gmail, GSuite, Yahoo and Mail.ru. If a user's email client doesn't support AMP emails, the recipient will see a plain TML fallback email we provide options for.
