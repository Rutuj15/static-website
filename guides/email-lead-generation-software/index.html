15 Email Lead Generation Software to Grow Your Leads in 2024

ByMailmodo

20 Dec, 2023

10 mins read

Email lead generation isn’t a one-size-fits-all process. You can generate leads in multiple ways. By including a sign-up form or a button in your email campaign, you can gain leads in the form of new subscribers. Another way, albeit controversial, is outbound lead generation. You could also place a display ad in another person's email to capture leads.

Like any other lead generation process, email lead gen requires the use of proper lead generation tools to attract prospects. That's why, in this guide, we share our top picks for email lead generation software.

15 Best Email Lead Generation Software to Try Out in 2024

This best lead generation software will help you land leads with little manual intervention.

Email prospecting/lookup

The first step in the email lead generation process is to find potential leads and create their database with the goal of communicating with them.

1. Zendesk Sell

Zendesk Sell is with you at every step of your prospecting process–be it lead qualification, lead management, or contact management. Its interface is simple and convenient to use, so your sales representatives can prospect hassle-free. Zendesk Sell is best suited for startups and enterprise-level businesses.

Read our guide on how to connect Zendesk with Mailmodo by using our Zendesk integration to supercharge your outreach email campaigns. Who wants to miss out on AMP components that drive up lead conversions by 41%?

Features:

  • Contact information database
  • Contact management
  • Data import/export

Zendesk Sell pricing: Suite Team $49 per agent/mo billed yearly, Suite Growth $79 per agent/mo billed yearly, Suite Professional $99 per agent/mo billed yearly

G2 rating: 4.2/5

2. Hunter

With Hunter.io, you can find professional email addresses and check their validity in bulk. The tool automatically identifies best-fit prospects for your sales team to go after. The team also provides services such as web research, email list subscriber building (specifically B2B lists), and scaling your existing lead generation efforts.

Features:

  • Autoresponders
  • List management
  • Reporting/analytics
  • Subscriber management
  • Template management

Hunter.io pricing: Free for 25 searches and 50 verifications/mo, paid plan $49/mo, Enterprise plan $399/mo

G2 rating: 4.4/5

3. Growbots

With Growbots, you can tap into a database of 200 million contacts separated by industry. The platform also enables you to test your outbound sales strategy to assess what works for your business and marketing strategies. Their customer support team will train you and your team in prospect status monitoring and field mapping to ensure you get quality leads.

Features:

  • Contact database
  • Data import/export
  • Lead capture
  • Pipeline management
  • Prospecting tools

Growbots pricing: Outreach plan $49/mo, all-in-one plan $199/mo

Frequently Asked Questions

It depends on your specific needs and goals. However, some top email lead generation software options include Zendesk Sell, Mailmodo, Hunter, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Growbots, and Aeroleads. Each of these platforms offers unique features and benefits such as customizable landing pages, email automation, and lead tracking. Ultimately, it's important to evaluate your business's budget, target audience, and marketing strategy to determine the best fit for your lead generation efforts.

If you're looking to grow your leads, there are several tools you can use for lead generation. One effective method is through email marketing, which involves crafting targeted messages to prospective customers. Cold email tools can also be useful in reaching out to individuals who may be interested in your product or service. Additionally, landing page forms can help capture leads by offering valuable gated content or incentives in exchange for contact information. By utilizing these tools and tactics, you can effectively generate more leads for your business.

An actionable and useful way to do this is by incorporating interactive elements in your emails. For example, you can include a referral form that asks for the referral contact information in exchange for the knowledge base and premium tools. Another option is to include a call-to-action button that leads to a landing page with a lead form.

The cost of lead generation software varies depending on the features and capabilities of the software. Some basic software can be found for free, while others may cost hundreds or even thousands of dollars per month. When considering the cost of lead generation software, it's important to evaluate the potential return on investment and how it aligns with your specific business goals.
