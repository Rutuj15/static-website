13 AI Email Marketing Tools To 10X Your Productivity

ByFalak Preet Kaur

10 Sep, 2024

17 mins read

Email marketing can be a handful and requires a lot of effort. AI for email marketing tools can help you maximize your efforts by providing automation services like copywriting, email personalization, and automating various email marketing tasks. This article will cover some of the best AI email marketing tools with a deeper analysis of how each tool works so you can figure out if it's the best bet for your business.

How to use AI for email marketing?

Email marketing is a hectic portion of marketing with much potential to be automated. Thanks to AI, this process can be automated and yield the best results from it.

  • Create compelling email copies

AI writing tools can be useful when you want to generate compelling email copies. These tools assist in writing personalized and eye-catching content for an email that can increase open rates.

  • Design stunning emails

To make your emails attractive and to add beautiful imagery to them, there are many AI design tools that you can make use of.

  • Automate email marketing tasks

Email automation tasks like sending, responding, and optimizing emails can be easily automated using AI automation tools. AI can also automate repetitive tasks that can make your job 10X easier.

  • Contact management

It's necessary to always build a healthy and consistent relationship with your audience or customers. Many AI contact management tools easily clean up, manage, and segment the data.

  • Send personalized emails

AI email personalization tools can assist in segmenting your email list and sending customized content per the users' tastes and preferences. AI can not only help with personalizing the first name but also the experience and content they will see. For instance, product recommendations are personalized for each segment.

  • Get email insights and inspiration

Many AI email marketing tools also track email campaigns quickly by collecting huge amounts of data and helping you discover what is working and what is not.
