No-code AI email marketing

Email marketing automation can hassle many marketers due to less coding experience and knowledge. These no-code AI tools can be useful for performing email marketing without coding.

1. Mailmodo

Mailmodo helps you put your email marketing efforts on autopilot by automating workflows and building interactive emails. It is a no-code email marketing builder to help you create effective email marketing campaigns.

Key features:

  • Insert dynamic content in your emails through live poll results, countdown timers, real-time inventory, account dashboards, and sports scores to reinvent your email campaigns.

Mailmodo is set to launch its AI integration soon, which will include the following:

  • Create and rewrite email copy like CTA button, paragraph, and links
  • Auto-generate / search images based on email content or text prompt
  • Subject-line and Preheader generator
  • Auto-generate sample templates based on user scenarios and text prompts.

Pricing:

BEEPro is a no-code AI tool that helps you quickly create beautiful, high-performing emails. This no-code AI email design tool provides several templates and lets you collaborate with your teammates.

No-code AI data scraping and predictive analytics

Data scraping and analytics require a lot of coding, which requires the help of data scientists or analysts. To make it easy, here's a list of some no-code AI tools for data analytics:

4. Octoparse

Octoparse is a no-code AI tool that helps you with web scraping. You can use this tool to extract data from websites without any coding knowledge. It helps you turn pages into structured data.

Key features:

  • You can create scraping tasks by selecting and setting up data fields, navigating through web pages, and setting up rules for data extraction.
  • Can gather data for market research, competitor analysis, and data analysis.
  • Provides functionalities like scheduling, cloud extraction, and data export options in one tool.

Pricing:

Free trial: ✅

Octoparse has a free plan that lets you set up only 10 tasks. You can opt for standard, professional, or enterprise plans to upgrade. The standard plan costs $75, with a free plan as well. Check all the plans here.

5. RapidMiner

RapidMiner is a low-code, no-code AI tool that helps you analyze data and build predictive models. Using various machine learning algorithms.

Key features:

  • Offers various tools for data preprocessing, modeling, and deployment of the models.
  • Provides interactive visualizations and reporting that help you understand and communicate insights from the data.
  • Offers advanced analytics, including text scraping, time series analysis, sentiment analysis, etc.

Pricing:

Free trial: ❌

RapidMiner does not provide a free trial, but you can request a demo and quote for pricing here.

6. Tableau

Tableau is a low-code, no-code AI tool focusing on data visualization, analytics, and reporting. It provides powerful features, including creating interactive visualizations, building insightful dashboards, and conducting data analysis.

Key features:

  • Because of its conversational and intuitive user interface, you can visualize, understand, and explore the data more effectively.
  • With Tableau's no-code approach, users can analyze data and create interactive dashboards without writing complex code.
  • Helps users understand why certain data patterns are happening through AI-driven results.

Pricing:

Free trial: ✅

Tableau has a free trial that lets you test out the product. It has creator, explorer, and viewer plans. You can quote the sales team for pricing and more.

No-code AI ML models creation

Having a developer by your side can be a plus while creating machine learning models, but it's not a necessity. These low-code AI tools can help you make ML models in minutes with limited coding expertise:

7. Obviously

Obviously is one of the most innovative no-code AI tools that lets you convert raw data into predictive models in minutes using its fast and cutting-edge AI-driven technology without any prior coding knowledge.

Key features:

  • Obviously lets you predict churns, lead conversions, loan repayments, frauds, etc.
  • You can upload your datasets, select a target and leverage its automated machine-learning capabilities to build accurate predictive models.
  • Provides integrations with various data sources and systems, making it easy to connect and analyze data from various sources.

Pricing:

Free trial: ✅

Obviously gives a free trial for learners which comes with only 1,000 predictions. If you want to upgrade, you can choose from limited and full-access plans with a free 14-day trial starting from $300 per month. Check all the plans here.

8. Metatext

Metatext is a no-code AI tool that lets you annotate, curate, build, deploy, and manage custom NLP datasets and models. It is an easy-to-use and no-code solution to your machine learning training.

Key features:

  • Simplifies the creation of customer AI and NLP solutions, even without any expertise in AI, data science, or coding.
  • Handles the heavy and time-consuming tasks for you through its intuitive UI and APIs.
  • You can integrate Metatext's APIs with your existing tools like Google Spreadsheets.
  • Automatically trains the best algorithm for your data; as you annotate, the AI continues learning and improving.

Pricing:

Free trial: ✅

Metatext has a free trial lets you create up to 2 projects and 1,000 predictions. You can try its pro and enterprise plans starting from $185 per month to scale. Check the features and pricing here.

9. Google Cloud AutoML

Google's AutoML is a low-code, no-code AI tool that enables developers with limited machine learning expertise to train high-quality models. It helps you create custom learning models in minutes.

Key features:

  • Prepare, store your datasets, and work with intuitive machine-learning tools that power Google.
  • You can experiment and deploy more models faster and manage them with confidence.
  • AutoML can handle a wide range of tabular information.

Pricing:

Free trial: ✅

AutoML comes with a free trial that gives you $300 credits which can be used for over 90 days. If you want to upgrade, you can contact the sales team. You can also use their pricing calculator for estimated costs.

14. Levity

Notion is a no-code AI tool that enables you to work faster by automating tedious and heavy tasks. It helps in automation and communicating with teammates effectively to get things done faster.

No-code AI Chatbots

Conversational AI chatbots can help you improve customer service with less money and time. These no-code AI chatbot tools can help you create and deploy chatbots in minutes:

18. Landbot

Landbot is a fun, intuitive, no-code conversational AI platform that empowers you to create any chatbot and deploy it anywhere. It has a visual chatbot builder and various templates to create your chatbot easily.

Key features:

  • You can connect or integrate any popular software platform using webhooks or APIs like WhatsApp, Hubspot, Slack, etc.
  • For beginners, it's super easy to deploy a chatbot as Landbot provides various templates to help you build the first chatbot in minutes.
  • You can create conversational marketing campaigns, engage existing customers and provide excellent customer service to your customers.

Pricing:

Free plan: ✅

This tool's Sandbox or free plan is limited to only 100 messages and is made for testing out. They have starter, pro, and business plans starting from $30 monthly if you want to upgrade. You can also claim discounts if you choose to pay yearly. For more information, visit their pricing page.

19. Poly

Poly AI is an AI conversational tool that helps you build a customer-led voice assistant and converse humanly. It helps you resolve over half of your customer calls and free your people for higher-value work.

Key features:

  • Eliminates wait times by providing 24/7 availability and improving resolution rates.
  • Human-like conversations that keep your customers engaged and easily solve their queries.
  • Helps you utilize relevant data by providing game-changing insights in real-time.

Pricing:

Free plan: ❌

Poly does not have a free trial or plan. However, you can request a demo of the tool before committing to the tool.

20. Yellow

Yellow is a no-code conversational AI platform that helps businesses build and deploy chatbots and virtual assistants for customer engagement and support purposes. This tool leverages AI to create interactive chat experiences for users.

Key features:

  • Incorporates advanced AI capabilities like machine learning and predictive analytics to improve the chatbot's performance continuously.
  • Capable of understanding and responding to user queries humanly.
  • Provides features such as context-aware conversations, dialogues, and sentiment analysis, ensuring a more human-like and personalized interaction.

Pricing:

Free plan: ✅

Yellow comes with a free plan with one chatbot and limited integrations. If you want to upgrade to its enterprise plan, including unlimited chatbots and other features, you must contact the sales team.

21. Chatfuel

Chatfuel is the biggest no-code platform used for creating and managing business messaging automation for Messenger, Instagram, WhatsApp, or your website

Based in the United States, Chatfuel heads the list of the best AI agent tools — autonomous AI-powered workers that can take over entire roles such as Sales representative, Support agent and others. The app offers advanced automation and a wide range of features that are popular with small and large brands alike.

Key features:

  • Conversational AI capabilities: Chatfuel’s integration with ChatGPT makes it a powerful tool for enhancing the customer experience. Using ChatGPT, your chatbots can effectively understand, respond to, and engage with customers in a human-like manner
  • Customer Segmentation & Targeted Communication: Chatfuel provides the capability to segment customers and send tailored communications to different groups
  • Integration options: Chatfuel seamlessly integrates with website platforms, popular CRMs (Customer relationship management), Google Sheets, other third-party applications
  • Pre-built customizable templates for different goals and scenarios: To save time, you can just duplicate Chatfuel’s templates and have your chatbot live in less than 30 minutes

Pricing:

  • Conversations based
  • Starts from $59 for WhatsApp and $15 for other platforms
  • Flexible custom plans from $300 for larger businesses available
