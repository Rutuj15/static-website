How to Add a Newsletter Popup to Your Shopify Store

ByMashkoor Alam

14 Jun, 2024

6 mins read

Newsletters are powerful tools for engaging visitors; however, building an email list of subscribers genuinely interested in hearing from you can be quite challenging. Sometimes, it’s difficult to find the right subscribers, and at other times, it’s difficult to convince your visitors to subscribe. So, how do you go about it?

Popups can be a viable solution. It is a good means of attracting visitors' attention. Once you have that, you can make them curious about your online store and give them a good reason to sign up for your email newsletter. You must have encountered websites and stores that proactively build an email list with newsletter popups. If you want to set up one for your Shopify store but don’t know how, you’ve come to the right place.

In this guide, we'll discuss the steps you should follow to create an email popup for your Shopify store and give you some inspirational newsletter popup examples of how some Shopify stores have implemented newsletter popups and why they work.

What is a newsletter popup in Shopify?

A newsletter popup in Shopify is a modal or overlay on a Shopify store's webpage. It prompts visitors to sign up for the store's email newsletter. These popups capture information like email address, name, age, gender, etc., from site visitors, allowing store owners to build their email lists for marketing purposes.

Why would you want to use a newsletter popup in Shopify?

Newsletter popups capture the attention of website visitors and persuade them to sign up for your email list. They often offer incentives like discount codes or exclusive content and resources in exchange for their emails. This encourages visitors to share their emails and helps you create a growing email list of subscribers. This list is a powerful asset for future marketing campaigns, allowing you to reach the target audience with targeted emails.

Additionally, as more visitors subscribe to your newsletter, you expand your email reach and grow a broader audience. This increased exposure is essential for building brand recognition and a strong market presence. A larger audience means more sales opportunities and business growth.

Furthermore, these popups also inform visitors about early access to sales, special events, and product launches. This increases engagement, which is necessary to make your special events successful.

How to create a newsletter popup for your Shopify store?

To create a newsletter popup for your Shopify store, you can explore third-party apps available in the Shopify App Store in your Shopify admin and install them. These apps help you to design a newsletter signup form and seamlessly integrate it into your website.

Once you have an email list, you must manage it consistently. This includes performing actions such as:

  • Updating entries

  • Deleting entries

  • Segmenting the email list

  • Creating and sending personalized emails

  • Setting up automation

Managing these actions across different platforms or tools can be overwhelming at times. Instead, you can use an ESP that offers comprehensive functionalities to manage your email list and create and send emails to your subscribers.

Let’s see how to create a newsletter popup for your Shopify store on Mailmodo.

How to add a newsletter popup with Mailmodo in Shopify?

Step 1: In the Mailmodo dashboard, click on Pages & Forms > Pop-up forms.

Step 2: Click on Create form to open the form builder.

Step 3: Choose how you want to create the pop-up form. You can select from one of the premade templates or start designing a pop-up form from scratch.

Step 4: Input the popup form's name and click Continue.

Step 5: Design your popup. Use elements such as “Count down timer,” “Add image,” and “Add video” present in the form builder. If you want advanced capabilities, use the “Code snippet” to add custom CSS and JS code. You can also change shade, size, and BG color to customize the form according to your preferences.

Step 6: Select fields such as email address, gender, or phone you want to capture. Select the list from the dropdown.

Step 7: Specify rules to trigger the popup. You can choose triggers like timing, page scroll, exit intent, etc.

Step 8: Now, choose where to publish your popup form. Enter the website URL and click on Connect.

Step 9: You’ll get a code to copy and paste into your website code before the tag.

Step 10: Test your popup on your website to make sure that it appears like you intended it to on different devices. Also, submit the form and check that it records the details correctly.
