8 Best Email Copywriting Examples for Inspiration

ByMashkoor Alam

07 Nov, 2024

6 mins read

In the digital marketing era, email marketing remains a preferred channel for marketers. Email copywriting is an indispensable skill, given the role it plays in persuasion in successful email marketing campaigns. Needless to say, if you nail your email copies each time, you can convert a potential customer to a repeat customer. This guide reviews a few of the best email copywriting examples and discusses why they work.

How to write an email copy that converts

Writing an email copy that converts requires strategic thinking and some specific things that you need to focus on. Here are some things that you should consider when writing a good email copy.

  • Identify the objective of your email

  • Choose a copywriting framework

  • Know your target audience

  • Make your email copy conversational

  • Write engaging subject lines

  • Use preheader texts

  • Keep your email copy short and relevant

  • Introduce yourself in the introduction

  • Tell a story

  • Make the copy digestible
