11 Best Email Warmup Tools of 2024

ByMashkoor Alam

31 Aug, 2024

10 mins read

Warming up your email account is key to ensuring your cold emails reach your recipients' inboxes rather than getting lost in their spam folders. Email warmup is especially important for new or inactive accounts because it improves the chances that your emails will be delivered and noticed by your audience.

An email warmup tool slowly increases the number of emails you send to help build a good reputation with email providers and improve your deliverability. But with so many options available, you might wonder how they differ from each other and which one would suit you the best.

In this article, we'll dive into the 11 best email warmup tools of 2024, exploring their features and pricing and how they can boost your email campaigns and help you achieve better results.

FAQs

You can technically start a cold email outreach campaign without warming up your email first, but it’s usually not a good idea.

If you’re using a new domain or suddenly send a lot of emails, your messages are likely to get flagged as spam. This will hurt your outreach efforts and prevent your emails from reaching your intended audience. While warming up your email might seem like a hassle, it's a crucial step for most salespeople and marketers.

Sure, you could technically use your new domain to send emails to your own inboxes, move them out of spam, mark them as important, and reply to them yourself.
