5 Best Email Drip Campaign Software 2024

ByAquibur Rahman

25 Sep, 2024

8 mins read

Email drip campaign best practices are fast becoming popular as they enable you to reach the right potential customers at the right time. There are multiple tools and software that enable you to achieve this. So how do you choose the best drip campaign software for your business? This guide covers a comprehensive review of 5 best email drip campaign softwares out there.

What is a drip campaign?

A drip campaign is a marketing method that allows automated sets of emails or 'drips' to be shared with customers based on specific timelines or user actions is called a drip campaign. It sends a sequence of pre-written emails to people who take a specific action on a website or application. For example, new subscribers will receive these emails after signing up. Later, they will receive time-scheduled emails until the campaign ends.

Learn more about drip campaigns, their use cases, and how to set them up in this guide.

How to send drip campaigns?

If you're wondering how to create an email drip campaign that works for your business, you only need a well-crafted message, a planned campaign, and tools to start the campaign. To send a drip campaign, you need to:

  • Identify and segment your audience.
  • Create the emails.
  • Set up the drip campaign.
  • Review and send the drip campaign.
  • Analyze the drip campaign performance.
