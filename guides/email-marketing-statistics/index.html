FAQs

Email marketing statistics are highly successful in demonstrating the effectiveness of marketing strategy, revealing that businesses can achieve significant ROI, strong engagement rates, and increased conversions. Data-driven insights help marketers refine strategies, optimize campaigns, and enhance customer relationships, making email marketing a powerful tool for driving business growth.

An email marketing benchmark is a standard or reference point that measures the performance of email campaigns against industry averages. These benchmarks include metrics such as open rates, click-through rates, conversion rates, and unsubscribe rates. They help marketers assess the effectiveness of their campaigns and identify areas for improvement.

Here are some key email marketing statistics to know:

  1. Open Rate: The average open rate across industries is around 20-30%.
  2. Click-Through Rate (CTR): Average CTR typically ranges from 2-5%.
  3. Conversion Rate: The average conversion rate for email marketing is about 1-3%.
  4. ROI: Email marketing can yield an average return of $42 for every $1 spent.
  5. Mobile Engagement: Over 50% of emails are opened on mobile devices.
