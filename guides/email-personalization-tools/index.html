6 Best Email Personalization Tools of 2024

ByManpreet Kaur

18 Sep, 2024

7 mins read

About 72% of customers say they engage with marketing messages personalized according to their interests and preferences. That shows how important personalization is. By sending personalized emails based on their preferences, businesses can drive engagement and get the most out of their email marketing efforts.

While personalizing emails for each customer can be effective, it becomes challenging and near-impossible to maintain that as your email list grows and you scale your email marketing efforts. This is where email personalization tools play an important role, streamlining the process and making it easy to manage big campaigns.

This guide contains a curated list of the 6 best email personalization tools that we've shortlisted, along with their key features and pricing so it becomes easy for you to take your pick.
