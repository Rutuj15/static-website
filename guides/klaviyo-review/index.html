Klaviyo Review 2024: An E-commerce Email Marketing Software

ByNupur Mittal

02 Sep, 2024

14 mins read

For e-commerce business owners, Klaviyo has established itself as the go-to ESP. With its top-notch features targeted at Shopify store owners and other e-commerce businesses, it has made its mark. Klaviyo offers some of the most impressive features, from email automation flows to AI-based product recommendations.

But, Klaviyo charges high prices for email and SMS marketing plans. This can be a major drawback if you seek an affordable ESP.

In this Klaviyo review, we will analyze the tool based on our experience and customer feedback. So, if you choose Klaviyo as your ESP, stay with us until the end.

What is Klaviyo?

Klaviyo is an e-commerce email marketing service provider that helps you create and scale email marketing campaigns. Along with email marketing, the tool also helps you market SMS and has dedicated features.

The most used features include a drag-and-drop template editor, sign-up, form builder, transactional emails, and a marketing automation builder. Each feature is superb and helps you segment and personalize your contact list to send relevant and targeted emails.

What are Klaviyo's core features?

Here is a complete analysis of Klaviyo's key features:

1. Email campaign builder

Klaviyo has a user-friendly drag-and-drop template builder. It has all the essential features to create and send engaging emails. There are three ways to create templates: Use an HTML email editor, a plain-text editor, and upload your HTML code.

There are 94 themed templates and 19 layout wireframe-based templates.

You can add conditional logic by specifying conditions to show/hide elements to certain users. This feature helps you create more relevant emails for the target recipient.

You can use Klaviyo's artificial intelligence assistant to help you.

  • Generate subject lines based on the email type

  • Analyze past best-performing subject lines to pick the highest-performing ones for future campaigns, and

  • Shares best practices for writing compelling and click-worthy subject lines

You can also run A/B testing and test these variables: Email content and send timings (date and time).

2. Email marketing automation

There are 61 premade emails, and e-commerce flows for different email campaign goals. Most flows use Shopify as a trigger, making it easy for e-commerce brands to run targeted email series. Here are the categories of goals available for different flows:

  • Prevent lost sales

  • Nurture subscribers

  • Build customer loyalty

  • Remind customer to purchase

  • Encourage repeat purchase

  • Update customers on order status

  • Others

The marketing automation builder seems easy to use and clear, even for beginners.

There are options to track UTM links, send time optimization features, and add advanced filters. These filters let you hyper-segment your flows by restricting people from a certain flow based on whether they fulfill the conditions mentioned by you.

Creating different variations of each email in the flow for A/B testing is also possible. Another interesting feature is the quiet hour feature if you trigger SMS in the marketing flows. The tool doesn't send an SMS during the specified hours. It helps ensure you are not pinging your recipient at the wrong time, ultimately impacting your campaign's performance.

3. Sign-up form builder

Klaviyo's form builder allows you to build four types of forms - pop-ups, embedded, full page, or fly-out. You'll find 60 premade templates in their template library, which you can filter by the type of form and goals you want to achieve. All these forms are available even if you use Klaviyo's free plan.

The SMS builder is one of the most impressive ESPs we have analyzed. There are many customization options to create compelling forms to meet your goals. For instance, you can add blocks influencing multi-select, coupon codes, dropdown menus, radio buttons, and more.

The targeting and behavioral options are also advanced, as you can configure them when showing the form. There is also an option to hide/show the form on desktop or mobile; you can configure which customers should see the form. These options help you target the right recipients and convert them.

The teaser feature, which gives you a preview of your form, is interactive. You can see how the form will look, make changes, and see the updated version in real-time.

Klaviyo's key strengths and weaknesses

Based on Klaviyo's customer reviews, we have highlighted the pros and cons of the tool:

Klaviyo Pros

  • Advanced yet user-friendly tool: They have kept it user-friendly while offering powerful and advanced features. Such a combination is rarely found among various ESPs.

  • Advanced sign-up form builder: Klaviyo's sign-up form builder is one of the ESPs we have analyzed so far. It offers multiple design and style options, and you can easily configure targeting and behavioral settings. Their teaser feature shows you how the form will look in real-time, and you can change it simultaneously.

  • List creation and segmentation: The tool offers you multiple ways (sign-up forms, 7 segment options with AND conditions to get even more specific with your segments.

The segmentation tool is phenomenal, given how many variables you can use to create specific lists of people for campaigns; we have even used these lists outside Klaviyo for integration with other platforms, such as attentive and custom loyalty-type incentives! - Cody B.

  • Detailed engagement reporting: Klaviyo offers engagement reports in its list and segmentation sections. You get engagement reports for different lists and segments, which are quite detailed. For instance, you can filter by different email clients and see members' engagement from very engaged to unengaged.

Klaviyo Cons

  • Expensive for its features: Even though Klaviyo's features are outstanding and help you create, manage, and scale your email marketing campaigns, the pricing seems unreasonable. Besides, they have put a limit on the monthly email sent on each plan, which means you need to pay extra for every additional email you send.

  • Slow customer service: Klaviyo's customer support service often takes longer to respond even though their responses are helpful and solve the customer's problem. They can improve this one.

Is Klaviyo the right tool for you?

Now, the big question. Should you use Klaviyo as your email service provider? We've listed key points to help you decide:

When it's recommended

  • If you want to run SMS marketing campaigns: The SMS marketing feature is easy to use, and you can send SMS triggers based on opt-ins, website behavior, or email activity. The whole process of setting up your first SMS campaign is seamless.

  • If you are e-commerce: Klaviyo is excellent for e-commerce businesses and has various options to scale your marketing campaigns. From premade Shopify-triggered workflows to predictive AI product recommendations, you get a complete suite of features for your e-commerce business. Moreover, you can easily use promotional and transactional SMS to boost your conversion rate.

Klaviyos features are much more advanced and a valuable tool for any eCommerce Business. - Gartner Peer Insights

  • If you use Shopify: Klaviyo advertises itself as the leading ecommerce platform for Shopify owners. Most workflow templates are based on Shopify integration, making sending relevant emails to visitors super easy.

When it's not

  • If you have a low budget: Klaviyo prices are higher, making it an expensive ESP if you don't have such a huge budget.

  • If you want to send interactive AMP emails: Klavyo doesn't support creating and sending interactive AMP emails. You can add dynamic elements, but if you want to add AMP email widgets such as forms, shopping carts, polls, calendars, etc., Mailmodo would be a better ESP to consider.

The verdict

In a nutshell, Klaviyo is one of the most effective ecommerce marketing software available. It offers features like an advanced automation builder and 61 premade workflows for use cases like increasing AOV, nurturing new customers, or winning back inactive or unengaged customers. Besides, its predictive AI-based product recommendation also makes it an attractive choice for e-commerce businesses.

Another interesting feature is their CRM, labeled 'profile' in their left navigation panel. It gives an overview of how your customers engage with your store, their past purchase history, and how much they've spent. All this offers a holistic view you can leverage to send and create a more targeted and personalized experience for your website visitors.

But, one key drawback is Klaviyo's unjustifiable pricing. It charges $110 for just 4.5 - 5k contacts, which can be turned off for businesses with low budgets.
