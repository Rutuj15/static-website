19 Conversational AI Tools for Simplifying User Interactions

ByFalak Preet Kaur

02 Oct, 2024

14 mins read

Conversational AI tools are not limited to customer service. Need transcriptions? They'll transcribe audio into text with remarkable accuracy. Oh, and let's not forget about their efficiency as personal assistants. They can automate tasks, schedule appointments, and provide helpful reminders.

We've rounded up tried-and-tested chatbots, text-to-speech, speech recognition, and voice-activated assistants that deepen user engagement while maintaining your brand voice.

How do conversational AI tools work?

Conversational AI tools are trained on large amounts of data, such as text and speech. This data is utilized to teach the tool to understand and process human language and behaviors. The system then uses this to interact with humans naturally. It constantly learns from interactions and improves the quality of these interactions quickly.

AI speech recognition tools

AI analyzes and transcribes audio and videos to better understand and keep track of meetings, conferences, and conversations. Here are some of the popular speech recognition AI tools you can use:

9. Fireflies

Fireflies is an AI notetaker and recorder that helps you record, transcribe, search, and analyze voice conversations. It automates your meeting notes so you can take advantage of important conversations.

Key features:

  • Fireflies have passed the accuracy benchmarks and can accurately transcribe recordings and audio files.
  • Lets you search for keywords, themes, and topics to save time and find what you are looking for.
  • Fireflies can accurately transcribe conversations in various accents across various industries.

Pricing:

Free plan: ‚úÖ

Fireflies has a free plan with limited transcription credits and only 800 minutes of storage. To upgrade, you can choose from pro, business, and enterprise plans starting from $18 monthly. You can get it at a 40% discount if you choose to pay yearly. Check the full pricing plan here.

10. Assembly

Assembly uses AI models to recognize speech, detect speakers, summarize speech, etc. The use cases for this tool include AI transcription and captions, summarization, and audio/video content moderation.

Key features:

  • Assembly is a feature-rich AI speech recognition tool, including speaker labels, custom spelling and vocabulary, and more.
  • Provides usage-based pricing to scale according to product demand and shift priorities.
  • Provides sentiment analysis, entity detection, profanity filtering, and topic detection.
  • If you have no coding knowledge, you can try out its no-code playground.

Pricing:

Free plan: ‚úÖ

With the free plan, you can transcribe 5 hours of audio/video per month. Several other plans charge you per second. Check the full pricing plan here.

11. Voicegain

Voicegain helps you transcribe and analyze meetings and videos accurately. You can deploy this tool in your private infra or use the cloud it offers. You can also train your data to build tailored models and get high accuracy.

Key features:

  • You can submit the audio transcription file by sharing the meeting browser link.
  • You can also upload pre-recorded video content.
  • Extracts topics, positive and negative segments of your meetings, and highlights in the transcription using sentiment analysis.

Text-to-speech conversational AI tools

AI voice generators or AI text-to-speech tools can help you save lots of time and budget with human-like voices and scalable content.

12. LOVO

LOVO is an AI voice generator and text-to-speech platform that lets you generate audio and videos for numerous purposes, including marketing, advertising, education, training, etc.

Key features:

  • Has 400+ human-like emotional voices with over 140 languages to choose from.
  • You can easily dub, edit, and add voiceovers, background music, and sound effects to the tool.
  • You can even create covers for your videos with its interactive art generator, create copy, add subtitles, and correct pronunciation.

Pricing:

Free plan: ‚úÖ

The 14-day free trial includes 20 minutes of video generation and 1 GB of storage. Upgrade to basic, pro, and pro+ plans starting from $25. You can save 25% if you choose to pay yearly. Check the features with pricing here.

13. Speechify

Speechify is one of the most popular text-to-speech APIs that turns written text into spoken words in a human-like voice.

Key features:

  • Its high-quality AI voices can read up to 9x faster than the average reading speed.
  • Anything you save to the Speechify library instantly syncs across devices so that you can listen to it anytime.
  • The reading voices sound natural and human-like and are available in over 50 languages.

Pricing:

Free plan: ‚úÖ

The free plan is only 2 days and includes 10 standard reading voices and limited features. You can unlock various other features and unlimited voices with its premium plan for $11.58 monthly. You can check the full pricing plan here.

14. VEED

VEED is an AI software that converts text to speech using an AI voice generator. You can use it for subtitles, translation, marketing, social media, e-learning, training meetings, conferences, and sales videos.

Key features:

  • Converts any text into speech and adds it to any video straight from your browser.
  • Provides realistic AI human voices, and you can edit, add subtitles and transcribe all in one tool.
  • VEED not only lets you convert text to voice but also allows you to use all the video editing tools to create professional-looking videos.

Pricing:

Free plan: ‚úÖ

The free plan lets you create unlimited 720p videos with a watermark, and you can only work with videos for up to 10 minutes. To scale, you can try its pro, business, and enterprise plans starting from $599 per month. Check all the plans here,

15. Murf

Murf provides a versatile text-to-speech AI voice generator that allows you to create studio-like professional-looking videos in minutes. You can also use video cloning, voice changer, and voiceover.

Key features:

  • Murf provides different voices for different professions like an author, product manager, marketer, podcaster, etc.
  • You can control pitches and voice and insert pauses as required.
  • Murf has over 120+ text-to-speech voices in over 20 languages.

Voice-activated AI virtual assistants

Voice-activated virtual assistants are AI-powered tools that can be installed on your smartphones, home speakers, and other platforms. These types of assistants can be super helpful for people with disabilities. Some of these popular virtual assistants are:

16. Alexa

Alexa is a voice-controlled virtual assistant developed by Amazon. It is primarily used through Amazon Echo devices but can be used on other devices, such as smartphones and tablets. Alexa uses NLP and artificial intelligence technologies to provide voice-activated services and functions to make your life easier.

17. Siri

Siri is a voice-activated virtual assistant developed by Apple. It is available on Apple devices such as iPhones, iPads, Macs, Apple Watches, and HomePods. Siri, like Alexa, also uses NLP and AI to provide various services, from calling to making notes.

18. Google Assistant

Google Assistant is a voice-controlled virtual assistant available on various devices, including smartphones, smart speakers, smart displays, smart TVs, etc. Google Assistant can help you search for anything using just your voice.

19. Replika

Replika is An AI companion created to be your companion, a mentor or partner who is always by your side and eager to learn. Replika is always ready to chat when you need an empathetic friend. The best thing about this AI companion is that it is all about you, meaning it sees the world through your eyes.
