The fintech (financial technology) industry comprises various software, technologies, and applications created to improve and automate conventional finance structures.

Major fintech models:

  • Cashless payments

  • Investment/ personal finance

  • Mobile banking -loans/lending

  • Virtual currency/crypto

  • Crowdfunding

Each fintech model has its own set of email requirements. We have created a few basic flows that most fintech models can utilize to automate their emails. Customize these email sequences for your business model and user journey.
