What is Open house email flow?

Most prospects have to see a house in real life before deciding to purchase. So, when there is an open house tour available, send them this sequence to get them to register for the tour and check out the house.

  • Inform people about open house tour availability

  • Get people to register for the tour

Recommended emails in this sequence: 4 emails

Optimum flow time: 1 week

Emails in this sequence

  • Email # 1: Open house announcement
  • Email # 2: Follow up
  • Email # 3: Reminder
  • Email # 4: Feedback email

Email # 1: Open house announcement

Send them a follow-up email with the same information as in email #1, as they may have missed the first one. If they have registered, send them a confirmation email.

ðŸ“© Why this email

A few days after sending the first two emails, send them this email to remind them about the upcoming tour.
