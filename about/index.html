We’re proud to be leading the email revolution

At Mailmodo, we help marketers create, send and automate beautiful and interactive emails that perform like no other.

So many creative ideas were trapped in old, complex ESPs. Now they have a way out.

Not taking action within emails is so last year.

Interactive emails perform 3x better on average. So why would anyone choose not to use them? We’ve been asking ourselves that question a lot. We think the #1 cause is that marketers and their ideas are still trapped in complex and limiting email platforms. And we’re here to help them save themselves.

Remember this clunky way of having CTAs in emails?

It’s been an exciting journey,
so far.

2018-19

AMP technology is released

Interactive emails become a possibility but still not easily available for marketers.

2021

Mailmodo Launches

We release a complete platform for marketers to create and run interactive emails hassle-free.

2022

YC & Sequoia Funding

Y Combinator & Sequoia notice the potential of Mailmodo and we get our first investment.

2024

Mailmodo 2.0

We go beyond AMP technology and launch interactivity for Apple Mail, too.
