What is TikTok cost per thousand impressions (CPM)?

TikTok Cost per mile (CPM) is a metric used to determine the cost of reaching 1,000 impressions (views) of an ad on TikTok. It is a standard metric that helps you to understand the cost-effectiveness of your campaign. Calculating your TikTok CPM can help you to effectively plan and implement various marketing strategies on the platform.

How to use the TikTok CPM calculator

To calculate TikTok CPM, enter your total ad spend on TikTok and the total number of impressions you got. The tool will instantly calculate your TikTok CPM.

TikTok CPM formula

TikTok CPM (Cost Per Mile) = (Total Ad Spend (in USD) for TikTok Ads/Total Impressions) × 1000

Understanding the TikTok CPM result

The TikTok CPM result provides insight into the cost-efficiency of your ad campaign. The ideal rate will depend on various factors such as your goals and industry.

  • A low TikTok CPM suggests that your ad is reaching a large audience at a relatively low cost, and that your content and targeting strategies are effective.
  • A high TikTok CPM implies that your are spending more money per thousand impressions, which may suggest that you need to optimize your content or targeting strategies.

When to calculate TikTok CPM

TikTok CPM should be calculated regularly to assess the effectiveness of your ad campaigns. Some situations where you should calculate TikTok CPM include:

  • After launching a new TikTok ad campaign to measure initial performance
  • To evaluate the cost-efficiency of different ad creatives, formats, or targeting options
  • When comparing the performance of TikTok ads against other advertising platforms
  • During budget reviews to ensure that ad spending aligns with ROI goals
  • To benchmark against industry standards or competitors' CPM rates

How to calculate TikTok CPM with example

Imagine that you spent $5,000 on a TikTok ad campaign that generated 500,000 impressions. To calculate CPM:

TikTok CPM (Cost Per Mile) = (Total Ad Spend (in USD) for TikTok Ads/Total Impressions) × 1000

CPM=(5,000/500,000)×1000 = 10

In this example, your TikTok CPM is $10, indicating that you spent $10 on average to reach every 1,000 viewers.

How to improve your TikTok CPM

You can improve your TikTok CPM by using the following strategies:

  • Refine audience targeting to focus on high-conversion segments.
  • Optimize ad creatives to increase engagement and relevance.
  • Use trending music and formats to align with TikTok's style.
  • Experiment with different ad formats, such as Spark Ads or In-Feed Ads.
  • Utilize TikTok’s analytics to monitor performance and adjust campaigns.
  • Leverage retargeting to reach users who have previously interacted with your content.
  • Test different bidding strategies to find the most cost-effective option.
