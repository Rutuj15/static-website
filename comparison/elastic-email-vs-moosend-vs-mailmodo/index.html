Mailmodo is the best alternative to Elastic Email because it has user-friendly email design features, manages your deliverability to boost your inbox placement rate, and provides 24/7 email, phone, and customer support.

Elastic Email is good for businesses looking to do email marketing on a small scale. At a large scale, however, the tool has shown deliverability issues. For boosting your inbox placement rate, consider using Elastic Email alternatives like Mailmodo to get a dedicated IP server and 1-on-1 consultation with a deliverability expert.

Mailmodo is far more superior than Elastic Email in terms of deliverability. The former offers warmed up domain and tools to authenticate your emails to boost your inbox placement rate.

You can use Moosend for free to send unlimited emails to 1,000 contacts. However, if the contact limit feels restricting, consider using Moosend alternatives like Mailmodo that comes with an affordable pricing plan.

We recommend you to use Moosend if you manage invitations via email marketing. But we don’t recommend the tool for businesses that need advanced automation. Instead, Moosend alternatives like Mailmodo offer custom multistep automation at a more affordable price.
