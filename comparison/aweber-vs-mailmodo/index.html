Is AWeber the right email marketing tool for you?

AWeber is recommended for you:

  • If you are a very small business looking to setup auto-responders
  • If you’ve experience in creating email templates from scratch
  • If you’re not low in time budget, and afford a learning curve

AWeber isn’t recommended for you:

  • If you send both B2B and B2C campaigns
  • If you want to create and send interactive emails without coding
  • If you’d help from a library of pre-built email templates and automation sequences
