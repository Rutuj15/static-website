Best Mailgun Alternatives for 2024

ByDevashish Bariko

01 May, 2024

Share

Picking the right substitute for Mailgun can be quite a task. With so many options out there, the challenge is not just in finding the best but one that also fits your budget perfectly.

Luckily, you can skip the heavy lifting. We've sifted through Mailgun and its 20+ alternatives to bring you a shortlist of the top 8 options that we believe will meet your needs.

Being part of the Mailmodo team, it's natural to assume we might favor our own solution. Of course, we will, as we genuinely believe in our product and would love for folks to check us out. But at the same time we understand that our tool might not align with every marketer's requirements. That’s precisely why we've curated this list — to ensure every marketer finds value in it. Should you have any concerns or notice any inaccuracies, don't hesitate to contact us at enquires@mailmodo.com.

Disclaimer: Pricing, features, and service details are subject to change. Please visit each platform's website for the latest information.

Don't settle for the ordinary. Go beyond Mailgun and explore Mailgun alternatives.

#2

Mailchimp Transactional Email (formerly Mandrill)

Price (25k email per month) : $20/month

Best for Industries

Ecommerce Finance SaaS

Best for Use case

Transactional Email

Mandrill is a paid add-on to Mailchimp that creates and sends scalable, personalized transactional emails. Founded in 2011, Mailchimp acquired it in 2016 as a transactional email platform for its users. This acquisition increased the cost per 25000 emails by 101%, and the platform was renamed Mailchimp transactional. Users looking for transactional emails can opt for this Mailgun alternative and suits for mid-sized businesses.

Why Mandrill is a better Mailgun Alternative

  • Pre-built templates: Mailchimp provides 100+ customizable email templates focused on different industries.
  • Deliverability: Mailchimp offers features like send-time optimization and A/B testing for better open rates.
  • Domain Support: Mailchimp supports multiple sending domains without an additional fee.
  • API: Mailchimp supports transactional email delivery at scale via API.
  • Tagging: Mailchimp allows tags for outbound emails with automated or custom tags for easy analytics.

#3

SendGrid

Free plan available

Price (2.5k contacts) : $15/month

Best for Industries

Developers Enterprises

Best for Use case

Transactional Marketing

SendGrid is a cloud-based email service that provides a reliable platform for sending transactional and promotional emails. The platform is best for developers and businesses. SendGrid was established in 2009 by Isaac Saldana, Jose Lopez, and Tim Jenkins, headquartered in Denver, Colorado, United States and acquired by Twilio in 2019. Users looking for a transactional email service platform can opt for SendGrid as Mailgun alternative.

Why is SendGrid a better Mailgun Alternative

  • Email editor: SendGrid has a drag & drop editor but it also allows you to code your emails for better control.
  • Transactional emails: SendGrid offers a robust email API to automate sending transactional emails at scale.
  • Automations: SendGrid has the feature to automated email sequences like a welcome series. It can be deployed by all basic triggers - time delay, email opens, etc.
  • Customer support: Email and Chat support are available in paid plans. Human support is only in SendGrid's premium plan.
  • Webpage forms: To capture leads from landing pages, SendGrid also offers a customizable signup form for your website.

MessageBird is a cloud communications platform that enables businesses to connect with customers through SMS, Voice, and Chat APIs. Messagebird rebranded as Bird in 2024, introduced new pricing plans, and is suitable for mid-sized businesses. This unicorn is a great transactional email tool as Mailgun alternative.

Why Bird is a better Mailgun Alternative

  • Omnichannel Marketing: Bird provides Email marketing, SMS marketing, Whatsapp marketing, and Call dialer.
  • Signup Forms: Bird provides signup forms to convert website visitors to subscribers with multi-channel opt-ins.

#6

Amazon SES

Price (25k email per month) : $2.5/month

Best for Industries

E-commerce SaaS Finance Healthcare

Best for Use case

Transactional Email

Amazon Simple Email Service(SES) is a simple transactional email service platform that is a part of Amazon Web Services(AWS) at a stripped-down cost. It launched in 2011 as an email delivery tool for Amazon.com but later expanded to others. This cost-effective transactional email service with adequate features is suitable for enterprise and is a better Mailgun alternative.

Why Amazon SES is a better Mailgun Alternative

  • Deliverability: Amazon SES provides at-a-glance reports on sending and delivery data, such as bounce rates, opens, and clicks, in a single interface in the SES console.
  • API Integration: Amazon SES provides seamless automation of transactional workflows via APIs.
  • Tagging: Amazon enables adding custom tags to your emails, enabling advanced tracking and analytics for transactional messages.

Additionally, Elastic email provides Email Verification that flags unreachable and invalid email addresses. Joshua Perina, Michael Findlater, and Michał Bochnak founded Elastic Email in 2010 with a common goal of making email delivery efficient. Elastic Email is suitable for enterprises. Recently launching a new email designer in 2024, Elastic Email is a better Mailgun alternative.

  • Automations: Elastic Email has basic email automation cababilities which can also be triggered via APIs.

#8

Brevo (formerly Sendinblue)

Free plan available

Price (2.5k contacts) : $37/month

Best for Industries

SMBs Ecommerce

Best for Use case

Transactional Automation

Brevo, formerly called SendinBlue, is a great relationship marketing and sales automation platform that offers email marketing and a customer relationship management suite for small—to medium-sized businesses. Armand Thiberge founded the platform in 2012 that focuses on reducing marketing efforts, making it a strong contender as Mailgun alternative in the market.

Why is Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) a better Mailgun Alternative

  • Email editor: Brevo offers an intuitive drag & drop editor for creating HTML emails.
  • Automations: Brevo provides a visual email journey builder to send automated campaigns that can be triggered via APIs and webhooks.
  • Integrations: Brevo has 50+ integrations available on their paid plans including WordPress, Intercom, Salesforce.
  • Customer support: Brevo provides 24/7 email and chat support. With human support available in their premium plans.
  • Landing page: Brevo has the ability to create and host landing pages.
  • Multi-channel: Brevo also has the ability to send SMS and WhatsApp notifications.
