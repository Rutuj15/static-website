ActiveCampaign takes a long time to load pages and emails. That may be due to slow or overloaded servers. It can be frustrating and not a pleasant feeling for any email marketer. To ace email marketing, businesses prefer ActiveCampaign alternatives like Mailmodo for instant sending and automating. You can send emails and download campaign reports without delays with Mailmodo.

According to Email Deliverability Report 2022, 11% of emails sent via ActiveCampaign go to spam, which adversely impacts deliverability. Email marketers, for this reason, prefer ActiveCampaign alternatives like Mailmodo for email deliverability management via domain warmup, ​​email list cleaning, and detailed deliverability reports. We also have email deliverability experts on board to help you land in your users’ inboxes.

Compared to other email marketing tools like Mailmodo, ActiveCampaign is expensive. It’s counted among the highest-priced email marketing platforms. However, its price-to-performance ratio is poor owing to low email deliverability rates, emails going to spam, and basic automation workflows. Mailmodo, on the other hand, is worth every penny as it is 3-4 times more affordable than ActiveCampaign and has a bevy of advanced reporting and automation features to offer.

Mailjet has a user-friendly, intuitive interface which makes it easy to use for email marketers with little experience in this field. However, its drag-and-drop editor is limited in terms of its offerings. Mailjet is suitable for an individual who only wants to carry out basic email marketing tasks, but for enterprises that want to run successful campaigns, we suggest going with Mailmodo. Mailmodo's WYSIWYG editor has rich media and text capabilities with unlimited live previews and all the templates have an auto-responsive design.

Mailjet meets most of the email marketing needs of small businesses, but it lacks customer support and functionality. If you’re looking for a full stack email marketing solution to send automated, triggered, transactional, marketing, static, interactive, and targeted emails, look no further than Mailmodo. Bring life to your emails with interactive widgets, powerful personalization and automation features, and diverse triggers and integrations.
