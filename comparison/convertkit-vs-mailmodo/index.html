FAQs

You can sign up for free on ConvertKit to send unlimited emails to 1,000 subscribers. However, the trial, besides list segmentation and audience insights, doesn't give you a taste of automation and advanced reporting. You can consider using a ConvertKit alternative like Mailmodo. Mailmodo also offers complete analytics and automation features in its trial plan.

ConvertKit is tailormade for content creators, but it receives a lot of flak for poor deliverability and lack of advanced insights. If you've faced a low inbox placement rate recently, you can consider switching to a ConvertKit alternative like Mailmodo to fix your low engagement history, poor domain reputation, and scammy email list.

Mailmodo is a better choice in terms of email deliverability. The platform helps boost your email deliverability by offering you expert consultations to clean your lists, setting up proper authentication protocols, and managing your suppression lists.

ConvertKit's prices are inflated as compared to competitors. For example, if you’re using ConvertKit, you end up paying 4x of what you would pay to Mailmodo for sending transactional and marketing emails. Mailmodo is a cost-effective alternative to ConvertKit in this respect.

To ensure that the data you wish to keep from ConvertKit will still be accessible when the migration is finished, import contact data into Mailmodo. You should import only clean lists into Mailmodo and email an engaged list on your first send.
