FAQs

You can use Moosend for free to send unlimited emails to 1,000 contacts. However, if the contact limit feels restricting, consider using Moosend alternatives like Mailmodo that comes with an affordable pricing plan.

We recommend you to use Moosend if you manage invitations via email marketing. But we don’t recommend the tool for businesses that need advanced automation. Instead, Moosend alternatives like Mailmodo offer custom multistep automation at a more affordable price.

Moosend’s spam rate is high enough to cause low email deliverability. Their users’ emails often end up in spam. You can achieve better email deliverability with Mailmodo's dedicated servers, consultation, and help with your domain warm-up.

To ensure that the data you wish to keep from Moosend will still be accessible when the migration is finished, import contact data into Mailmodo. You should import only clean lists into Mailmodo and email an engaged list on your first send.
