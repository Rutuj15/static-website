Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the product and billing.

Klaviyo offers a free trial. You can create an account, edit and customize email templates, import and sync your data, and send emails with it. You can send a maximum of 500 emails to up to 250 contacts. You also receive 150 SMS credits. Once you exceed that limit, you will receive a prompt to upgrade your account by paying a fee.

Klaviyo was designed keeping eCommerce businesses in mind, while Mailmodo is an email marketing platform that caters to all kinds of organizations. For businesses that don’t sell physical products online, like service-based companies, Mailmodo has a range of features, such as AMP email interactivity and integrations with Hubspot, Google Sheets, Influencer Bit, and Pabbly to help you scale up.

Mailmodo is a better choice in terms of email deliverability. If you’re a Klaviyo user, your fluctuating email deliverability rates may be attributed to a low engagement history, poor domain reputation, or scammy email list. Mailmodo helps boost your email deliverability by giving you expert consultations, helping you with domain warm up, and removing spammers from our platform.

With Getresponse, you can send unlimited newsletters to 500 contacts free of cost. It also lets you connect your domain during the 30-day free trial. But without domain warmup and tracking analytics, this free trial is quite limiting for small businesses. A better alternative would be to sign up with Mailmodo. Besides unlimited templates and campaigns, it also provides you the complete analytics suite. Track opens, clicks, bounce, submissions, and unsubscribes at no cost.

If you're an individual looking to start a newsletter, then Getresponse is a suitable email marketing platform for you. During the 30-day free trial, you can experiment using the drag-and-drop creator and predesigned email templates. But if you're a small business looking to kickstart email marketing, use other Getresponse alternatives. Mailmodo, for example, lets you track your emails, improve your deliverability, and warm up your domain to help you get more opens, clicks, and subscribes.

Emails from GetResponse are known to land in the Promotions tab in the user's inbox. Besides, its limited pricing plans and poor deliverability encourage users to look for alternatives. Email marketing platforms like Mailmodo offer expert deliverability consultation to ensure your emails reach your customers' inboxes every time. Tap into the Mailmodo SMTP, that’s consistently warmed up for higher email deliverability.
