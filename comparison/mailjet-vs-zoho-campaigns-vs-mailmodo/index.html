Mailjet has a user-friendly, intuitive interface which makes it easy to use for email marketers with little experience in this field. However, its drag-and-drop editor is limited in terms of its offerings. Mailjet is suitable for an individual who only wants to carry out basic email marketing tasks, but for enterprises that want to run successful campaigns, we suggest going with Mailmodo. Mailmodo's WYSIWYG editor has rich media and text capabilities with unlimited live previews and all the templates have an auto-responsive design.

Mailjet meets most of the email marketing needs of small businesses, but it lacks customer support and functionality. If you’re looking for a full stack email marketing solution to send automated, triggered, transactional, marketing, static, interactive, and targeted emails, look no further than Mailmodo. Bring life to your emails with interactive widgets, powerful personalization and automation features, and diverse triggers and integrations.

You can use Mailjet for free forever, sending 6,000 emails/month. But they cap it at 200 emails/day, which makes the plan restrictive. If you want a flexible pricing plan for your email marketing needs, try Mailmodo. We don’t even ask for a credit card. You can send up to 10,000 emails to 10,000 contacts monthly with our free plan. It also comes with additional perks, like unlimited campaigns and advanced analytics reporting.

Zoho Campaign offers a free trial for 14 days where you can access all the features and send up to 6,000 emails to 2,000 contacts. However, if you find the trial plan too brief and are looking to get your skin in the email marketing game, you can consider taking advantage of Mailmodo’s free trial plan.

If you’re not able to build complex workflows with Zoho Campaigns, you’re not alone. A lot of customers have complained about its limited automation capabilities. If you’re looking for an automation solution which offers you pre-made workflows, custom triggers, and endless integrations, consider using Mailmodo.
